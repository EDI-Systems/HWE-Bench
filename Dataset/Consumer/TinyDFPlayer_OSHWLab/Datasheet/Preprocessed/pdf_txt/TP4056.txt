Component ID: TP4056
Component Type: Single-Cell Lithium-Ion Battery Linear Charger IC
Description: The TP4056 is a complete constant-current/constant-voltage linear charger for single-cell lithium-ion batteries. It integrates a power MOSFET, reverse-blocking diode, and current sensing, requiring minimal external components. It features programmable charging current up to 1A, a fixed 4.2V float voltage, automatic charge termination (C/10), and automatic recharge. Additional functions include thermal regulation, under-voltage lockout (UVLO), battery temperature monitoring, and status indication via two open-drain outputs. It operates from a USB or adapter power supply and enters a low-current sleep mode when input power is removed.

Key Parameters:
- **Maximum Charging Current**: 1000 mA (Programmable via external resistor)
- **Float (Charge) Voltage**: 4.2 V (±1% accuracy)
- **Input Supply Voltage (VCC) Range**: 4.0 V to 6.5 V (Operating)
- **Battery Under-Voltage Trickle Charge Threshold**: 2.9 V (Typical)
- **Charge Termination Threshold**: C/10 (Current falls to 1/10 of programmed value)
- **Standby Quiescent Current**: 50 µA (Typical)
- **Shutdown Quiescent Current**: 35 µA (Typical)
- **Battery Reverse Leakage Current**: 1 µA (Typical, Sleep Mode, VCC=0V)
- **Thermal Regulation**: Yes (Reduces charge current to limit die temperature)
- **Operating Ambient Temperature**: -40°C to +85°C
