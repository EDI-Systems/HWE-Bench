Component ID: DW01A
Component Type: Single-Cell Lithium-Ion/Polymer Battery Protection IC
Description:
The DW01A is a protection IC designed for single-cell lithium-ion or lithium-polymer battery packs. It monitors battery voltage and current to provide protection against overcharge, overdischarge, overcurrent (both charge and discharge), and short-circuit conditions. It achieves this by controlling external N-channel MOSFETs in series with the battery, features built-in protection delay timers, and supports charging from 0V.

Key Parameters:
- Overcharge Protection Voltage (VOCP): 4.28V ±50mV
- Overcharge Release Voltage (VOCR): 4.08V ±50mV
- Overdischarge Protection Voltage (VODP): 2.40V ±100mV
- Overdischarge Release Voltage (VODR): 3.00V ±100mV
- Discharge Overcurrent Protection Threshold (VEC1): 160mV ±20mV
- Short-Circuit Protection Threshold (VSHORT): 1.00V ±300mV
- Charge Overcurrent Protection Threshold (VCHA): -0.150V
- Operating Current (Typical): 1.5 µA
- Operating Temperature Range: -40°C to +85°C
- Package: SOT-23-6
