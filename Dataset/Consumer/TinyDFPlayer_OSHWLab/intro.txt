The **TinyDFPlayer** is a compact, battery-powered MP3 player designed around the ATtiny85 microcontroller and DFPlayer Mini module, prioritizing portability, simplicity, and reliability for personal audio use. Its core design思路 (design philosophy) integrates essential audio, power, and user interaction components into a small form factor: it uses the DFPlayer Mini for hardware MP3 decoding (supporting FAT16/FAT32 Micro-SD cards, folder-based playback), pairs with a TP4056 LiPo charger (4.2V/1A max) + DW01A/FS8205 battery protection system (for safe charging/discharging), and includes a 128x64 OLED display, wheel potentiometer (volume), 3 push buttons (playback control), and EEPROM (to retain the last played track). Functionally, it enables untethered audio playback via a 3.7V LiPo battery and 3W/4Ω speaker, with the ATtiny85 coordinating all components—delivering a user-friendly, open-source solution for portable music playback, with folder-based track organization (up to 99 folders/255 tracks) and seamless resume functionality.
