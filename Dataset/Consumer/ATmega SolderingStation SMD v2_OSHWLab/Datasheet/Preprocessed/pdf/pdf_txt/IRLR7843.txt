Component ID: IRLR
Component Type: N-Channel Power MOSFET
Description:
This is a high-performance N-channel power MOSFET designed for high-frequency switching applications, particularly in synchronous buck converters for computer processor power supplies and isolated DC-DC converters for telecom and industrial use. It features a very low on-state resistance (RDS(on)), ultra-low gate impedance, and is fully characterized for avalanche energy, making it suitable for efficient, high-current power conversion.

Key Parameters:
- Drain-to-Source Voltage (VDSS): 30 V
- Static Drain-to-Source On-Resistance (RDS(on)max): 3.3 mΩ (max at VGS = 10V, ID = 15A)
- Total Gate Charge (Qg): 34 nC (typ)
- Continuous Drain Current (ID): 161 A (max at TC = 25°C, VGS = 10V)
- Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 620 A
- Gate Threshold Voltage (VGS(th)): 1.4 V to 2.3 V
- Maximum Power Dissipation (PD): 140 W (at TC = 25°C)
- Forward Transconductance (gfs): 37 S (min)
- Junction-to-Case Thermal Resistance (RθJC): 1.05 °C/W (max)
- Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 to +175 °C
