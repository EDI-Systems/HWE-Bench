Component ID: SW1
Component Type: Incremental Rotary Encoder with Push Switch
Description: The SW1 is an incremental rotary encoder with an integrated momentary push button. It converts rotational movement into quadrature digital pulses on phases A and B to detect both direction and displacement. The common terminal C is used as a ground reference for the encoder signals. The integrated switch provides a normally open contact between pins 1 and 2, typically used for menu selection or power control.

Key Parameters:
- Resolution 20 pulses per revolution
- Rotation 360 degrees continuous
- Switch Type Momentary Push Button
- Rating 10mA at 5V DC
- Contact Resistance 100m ohm maximum
- Operating Temperature -30 to +70 Celsius
- Rotational Life 30000 cycles
