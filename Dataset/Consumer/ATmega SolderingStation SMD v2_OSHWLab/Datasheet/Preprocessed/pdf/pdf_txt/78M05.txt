Component ID: 78M05
Component Type: Positive Voltage Linear Regulator (LDO)
Description: The 78M05 is a monolithic positive voltage regulator in a TO-252 (DPAK) package, designed to provide a fixed 5V output from a higher input voltage. It features internal thermal overload protection and short-circuit current limiting, making it suitable for local on-card regulation to eliminate noise and distribution problems associated with single-point regulation.

Key Parameters:
- Fixed Output Voltage (Vo): 5.0 V (Typical, 4.75V to 5.25V range)
- Maximum Input Voltage (VIN): 35 V
- Maximum Output Current (IO): 500 mA
- Dropout Voltage (VD): 2 V (Typical, at IO=350mA)
- Line Regulation (delta VO): 100 mV (Max, VIN=7V to 25V, IO=200mA)
- Load Regulation (delta VO): 100 mV (Max, VIN=10V, IO=5mA to 500mA)
- Quiescent Current (IQ): 6 mA (Max)
- Operating Junction Temperature (TOPR): -40°C to +125°C
- Thermal Resistance, Junction-to-Air (RthJA): 100 °C/W
