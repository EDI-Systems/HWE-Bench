Component ID: LMV358
Component Type: Dual Low-Voltage Rail-to-Rail Output Operational Amplifier
Description: The LMV358IDR is a dual-channel, low-power operational amplifier featuring rail-to-rail output voltage swing. It is designed to operate from a supply voltage as low as 2.7V up to 5.5V, making it ideal for low-voltage, battery-powered portable electronics and sensor signal conditioning.

Key Parameters:
- Supply Voltage Range: 2.7V to 5.5V
- Gain Bandwidth Product (Typical): 1MHz
- Quiescent Current (Typical): 210uA (for both channels)
- Input Offset Voltage (Typical): 1.7mV
- Input Bias Current (Typical): 11nA
- Output Swing: Rail-to-Rail
- Operating Temperature Range: -40 to 125 Celsius
