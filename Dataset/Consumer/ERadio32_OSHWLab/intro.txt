Please design a portable wireless smart audio player and network radio. The core architecture must feature a wireless central processing unit, a comprehensive audio decoding and amplification pipeline, a rich local human-machine interface, and a robust portable power management system. The power system should support single-cell rechargeable battery management alongside a soft-latching power switch topology and stable step-down regulation to ensure clean power delivery to sensitive audio and radio frequency circuits. 

The operational workflow begins with the power management system providing a stable logic voltage from either an external charging source or the internal battery. Once booted, the wireless processor fetches digital audio streams via network connectivity or reads them from local removable storage. The audio data is either processed internally or routed to a dedicated hardware decoding module to handle various high-fidelity formats. The decoded digital audio stream is then transmitted to dual digital class-D amplifiers to directly drive stereo speakers, as well as an auxiliary digital-to-analog converter for external line output. Concurrently, the central processor handles user interactions by driving a color graphical display for visual feedback, while receiving input commands from a rotary navigation encoder and an infrared remote receiver, ensuring a seamless and interactive playback experience.
