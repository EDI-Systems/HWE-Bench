Component ID: ESP32-WROVER
Component Type: Wi-Fi + Bluetooth + Bluetooth LE System-on-Chip (SoC) Module
Description:
The ESP32-WROVER-E and ESP32-WROVER-IE are highly-integrated, general-purpose Wi-Fi + Bluetooth + Bluetooth LE MCU modules. They are built around the ESP32-D0WD-V3 dual-core Xtensa® LX6 microprocessor, featuring integrated 4 MB SPI flash and 8 MB SPI PSRAM. The modules support IEEE 802.11 b/g/n Wi-Fi (up to 150 Mbps) and Bluetooth v4.2 BR/EDR & BLE standards. Designed for low-power applications, they include a rich set of peripherals (GPIO, ADC, DAC, SPI, I2C, I2S, UART, PWM, touch sensors, etc.) and are capable of handling demanding tasks like audio processing. The primary difference between the -E and -IE variants is the antenna: the -E uses a PCB antenna, while the -IE uses a connector for an external antenna.

Key Parameters:
- Core: Dual-core Xtensa® LX6 microprocessor
- CPU Clock Speed: 80 MHz to 240 MHz (adjustable)
- Integrated Flash Memory: 4 MB SPI Flash
- Integrated PSRAM: 8 MB SPI PSRAM
- Operating Voltage: 3.0 V to 3.6 V
- Wi-Fi Protocol: 802.11 b/g/n (802.11n up to 150 Mbps)
- Bluetooth Protocol: Bluetooth v4.2 BR/EDR and BLE
- Operating Temperature Range: -40°C to 85°C
- Sleep Current: < 5 µA
- RF Output Power (Typical): Up to 20 dBm
- Wi-Fi Receiver Sensitivity: -97 dBm (typical for Bluetooth BLE)
