Component ID: AMS1117
Component Type: Low Dropout (LDO) Linear Voltage Regulator
Description:
The AMS1117 is a family of three-terminal, positive, low-dropout linear voltage regulators capable of providing up to 1A of output current. It is available in both adjustable and fixed output voltage versions (1.5V, 1.8V, 2.5V, 2.85V, 3.3V, 5.0V). The device is designed to operate with a low input-to-output voltage differential, featuring a guaranteed maximum dropout voltage of 1.3V at full load, which decreases at lower currents. It includes on-chip trimming for reference voltage accuracy and built-in current limiting for overload protection.

Key Parameters:
- Output Current: 1A (Max)
- Dropout Voltage: 1.3V (Max at 1A), typical operation down to 1V differential
- Line Regulation: 0.2% (Max)
- Load Regulation: 0.4% (Max)
- Output Voltage Options: Fixed: 1.5V, 1.8V, 2.5V, 2.85V, 3.3V, 5.0V; Adjustable: 1.25V reference
- Input Voltage (Absolute Maximum): 15V
- Operating Junction Temperature: -40°C to 125°C
- Package Options: SOT-223, TO-252 (DPAK), 8L SOIC
