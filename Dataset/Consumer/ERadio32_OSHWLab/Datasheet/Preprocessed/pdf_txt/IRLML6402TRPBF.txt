Component ID: IRLM
Component Type: P-Channel Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET
Description:
This is a P-Channel MOSFET utilizing advanced trench technology, designed for efficient power switching in a compact SOT-23 package. Its primary function is to act as a voltage-controlled switch, where a negative gate-source voltage turns the device on, allowing a high continuous drain current (up to -4.1A) to flow with a very low on-state resistance. It is characterized by excellent RDS(on) performance and low gate charge, making it suitable for applications like load/power switching, interfacing, and logic level shifting.

Key Parameters:
- Drain-Source Voltage (VDSS): -20 V
- Continuous Drain Current (ID): -4.1 A (Tc=25°C)
- Drain-Source On-State Resistance (RDS(on)): < 52 mΩ (Typ 39 mΩ) @ VGS = -4.5V, ID = -4.1A
- Gate Threshold Voltage (VGS(th)): -0.7 V (Typ), Range: -0.45 V to -1.0 V
- Total Gate Charge (Qg): 7.8 nC (Typ)
- Maximum Power Dissipation (PD): 1.7 W
- Input Capacitance (Ciss): 740 pF (Typ)
- Thermal Resistance, Junction-to-Ambient (RθJA): 74 °C/W
