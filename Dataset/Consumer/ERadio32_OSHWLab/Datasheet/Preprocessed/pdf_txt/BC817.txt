Component ID: BC817
Component Type: NPN Bipolar Junction Transistor (BJT)
Description:
The BC817 is a general-purpose NPN bipolar junction transistor designed for medium-power amplification and switching applications. It features a high collector current capability, high DC current gain, and a low collector-emitter saturation voltage, making it suitable for audio frequency (AF) amplification and switching circuits requiring collector currents up to 500mA.

Key Parameters:
- Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 45 V (Max)
- Collector Current (Ic Continuous): 500 mA (Max)
- Collector Power Dissipation (Pc): 300 mW (Max)
- DC Current Gain (hFE): 100-250 (BC817-16), 160-400 (BC817-25), 250-600 (BC817-40) @ Vce=10V, Ic=150mA
- Collector-Emitter Saturation Voltage (VCE(sat)): 0.7 V (Max) @ Ic=500mA, Ib=50mA
- Transition Frequency (fT): 100 MHz (Min) @ Vce=5V, Ic=10mA, f=100MHz
- Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 50 V (Max)
- Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 5 V (Max)
- Junction/Storage Temperature (Tj, Tstg): -55 to +150 °C
