Component ID: USB
Component Type: 6-Pin USB Type-C Receptacle (Power/Charging Only)
Description: The TYPE-C-31-M-17 is a specialized USB Type-C receptacle designed primarily for power delivery and charging applications. It simplifies the standard Type-C interface by providing only the essential pins for power (VBUS, GND) and orientation/negotiation (CC1, CC2), omitting all data lines. This makes it an ideal, cost-effective choice for devices that do not require data transfer, such as rechargeable flashlights, simple power banks, or battery-powered toys.

Key Parameters:
- Pin Count: 6 functional signal pins + 4 mounting/shield pins
- Interface: Power Only (No USB 2.0 or 3.x data)
- Power Compatibility: Supports USB Power Delivery (PD) negotiation via CC pins
- Mounting Type: Surface Mount (SMT) with through-hole shell tabs for mechanical strength
