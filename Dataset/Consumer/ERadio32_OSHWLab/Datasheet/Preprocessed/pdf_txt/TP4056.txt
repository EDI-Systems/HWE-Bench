Component ID: TP4056
Component Type: Single-Cell Lithium-Ion Battery Linear Charger IC
Description:
The TP4056 is a complete constant-current/constant-voltage linear charger for single-cell lithium-ion batteries. It integrates a power MOSFET, reverse-blocking diode, and current sense resistor, requiring minimal external components. It features programmable charge current up to 1A, a fixed 4.2V float voltage, and automatic charge termination when the current drops to 1/10 of the set value. Additional functions include thermal regulation, battery temperature monitoring, under-voltage lockout, automatic recharge, and status indication via two open-drain outputs.

Key Parameters:
- **Programmable Charge Current**: Up to 1000 mA (set via external resistor R_PROG)
- **Float Voltage**: 4.2V (±1% accuracy)
- **Input Supply Voltage (VCC)**: 4.0V to 6.5V (operating range)
- **Battery End-of-Charge Termination Threshold**: C/10 (Charge current falls to 1/10 of programmed value)
- **Trickle Charge Threshold Voltage**: 2.9V (typical)
- **Trickle Charge Current**: Programmable as 1/10 of the full-scale current (e.g., 100 mA for 1A setting)
- **Standby Quiescent Current**: 50 µA (typical)
- **Shutdown Quiescent Current**: 35 µA (typical)
- **Thermal Regulation**: Yes, reduces charge current to limit die temperature
- **Operating Ambient Temperature**: -40°C to +85°C
