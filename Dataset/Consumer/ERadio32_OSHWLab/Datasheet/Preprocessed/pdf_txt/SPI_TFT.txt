Component ID: SPI_TFT
Component Type: 2.8-inch TFT LCD Module with Touch and SD Card
Description: This is a high-resolution 2.8" color TFT display module, featuring a 240x320 pixel resolution. It typically utilizes the ILI9341 controller and communicates over a Serial Peripheral Interface (SPI). This module is highly integrated, combining three separate SPI devices into a single board: the main LCD, a resistive touch controller (usually the XPT2046), and a standard SD card socket. This makes it ideal for developing comprehensive graphical user interfaces in embedded systems where space is at a premium.


Key Parameters:
- Screen Size: 2.8 inches
- Resolution: 240 x 320 Pixels
- LCD Controller: ILI9341 (common)
- Touch Controller: XPT2046 (Resistive)
- Interface: SPI (separate chip selects for LCD, Touch, and SD)
- Operating Voltage: 3.3V (Logic) / 3.3V to 5V (VCC)
