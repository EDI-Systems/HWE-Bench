Component ID: TLV704
Component Type: Low-Dropout (LDO) Linear Voltage Regulator
Description:
The TLV704 series are CMOS-based low-dropout linear regulators designed to provide a stable, fixed output voltage from a higher input voltage. Key characteristics include a high maximum input voltage of 30V, very low quiescent current for power-sensitive applications, and a low dropout voltage, making them suitable for battery-powered and communication equipment where input voltage can be close to the desired output.

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Input Voltage (VIN): 30 V
- Maximum Output Current (IOUT): 150 mA
- Dropout Voltage (VDIF): 30 mV (Typical, at IOUT=1mA, ΔVOUT=2%)
- Quiescent Current (IQ/ISS): 1.8 µA (Typical)
- Output Voltage Accuracy (Tolerance): ±2%
- Output Voltage Options (VOUT): 3.0V, 3.3V, 5.0V (Fixed, model-dependent)
- Operating Temperature Range (TA): -40°C to +85°C
- Thermal Resistance (θJA): 500 °C/W (SOT-23-5L package)
- Maximum Power Dissipation (Pw): 200 mW (SOT-23-5L package)
