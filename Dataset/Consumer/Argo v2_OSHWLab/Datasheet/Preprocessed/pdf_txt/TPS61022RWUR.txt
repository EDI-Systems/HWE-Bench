Component ID: TPS61022
Component Type: Boost Converter (DC-DC Switching Regulator)
Description: The TPS61022 is a high-efficiency, monolithic synchronous boost converter designed to generate a stable output voltage (2.2 V to 5.5 V) from an extremely low input voltage source (0.5 V to 5.5 V). It integrates two power MOSFETs, operates at a high switching frequency (0.6 MHz to 1 MHz), and is optimized for applications like supercapacitor backup and battery-powered devices where the input can dip very low. It features selectable power-saving modes, comprehensive protection, and a very small solution size.

Key Parameters:
- Input Voltage Range: 0.5 V to 5.5 V
- Output Voltage Setting Range: 2.2 V to 5.5 V
- Switch Current Limit (Valley): 6.5 A (min)
- Switching Frequency: 1 MHz (VIN > 1.5 V), 0.6 MHz (VIN < 1 V)
- Quiescent Current: 26 µA (Light Load)
- Peak Efficiency: 94.7% (at VIN=3.6V, VOUT=5V, IOUT=3A)
- MOSFET On-Resistance: 12 mΩ (LS) / 18 mΩ (HS)
- Operating Junction Temperature: -40°C to 150°C
