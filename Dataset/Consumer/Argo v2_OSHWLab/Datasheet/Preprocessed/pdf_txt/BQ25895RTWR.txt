Component ID: bq25895
Component Type: I2C-Controlled Single-Cell Li-Ion/Li-Polymer Battery Charger and Power Path Manager with Boost Converter
Description:
The bq25895 is a highly integrated switch-mode battery charge management and system power path management IC designed for single-cell Li-Ion/Li-Polymer batteries. It features a 5-A buck charger optimized for high-voltage inputs (up to 14 V) and includes a boost converter capable of delivering up to 3.1 A from the battery to the system. The device supports Narrow-VDC (NVDC) power path management, allowing the system to operate instantly with or without a battery. It integrates all necessary MOSFETs, current sensing, and control logic, and is configurable via I2C for parameters like input current limit, charge voltage/current, and boost output voltage. Key features include USB input detection (SDP, CDP, DCP), Input Current Optimizer (ICO), battery temperature monitoring, and thermal regulation.

Key Parameters:
- **Charge Topology**: Switch-mode buck converter
- **Max Charge Current**: 5 A
- **Input Voltage Range**: 3.9 V to 14 V
- **Battery Charge Voltage Regulation Accuracy**: ±0.5%
- **Boost Converter Output Voltage Range**: 4.5 V to 5.5 V (adjustable)
- **Max Boost Output Current**: 3.1 A
- **Switching Frequency (Charge)**: 1.5 MHz (fixed)
- **Switching Frequency (Boost)**: 500 kHz to 1.5 MHz (selectable)
- **Battery Discharge MOSFET Rds(on)**: 11 mΩ
- **Quiescent Current (Low Battery Leakage)**: 12 µA
