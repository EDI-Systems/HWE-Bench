Component ID: TF-123
Component Type: Micro-SD (TF) Card Connector
Description: The TF-123-ARP9H15 is a standard Micro-SD (TransFlash) card receptacle designed for surface-mount (SMT) PCB applications. It provides the physical and electrical interface for Micro-SD storage cards, supporting both high-speed SDIO and simpler SPI communication protocols. This component typically features a mechanical locking mechanism (such as push-push or push-pull) and an integrated switch for hardware-level card detection.

- **Mounting Type:** Surface Mount (SMT), Right Angle
