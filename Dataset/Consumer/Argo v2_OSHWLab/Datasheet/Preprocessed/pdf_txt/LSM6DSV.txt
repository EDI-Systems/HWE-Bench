Component ID: LSM6DSV16XTR
Component Type: iNEMO 6-axis Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU)
Description: The LSM6DSV16XTR is a flagship-grade, ultra-low-power 6-axis IMU from STMicroelectronics. It integrates a 3D digital accelerometer and a 3D digital gyroscope with a high-performance sensor hub and advanced digital features. Notably, it includes "Qvar" (charge variation detection) for user interface applications (like skin contact or gesture sensing) and dual-channel OIS (Optical Image Stabilization) support. It is designed for high-end smartphones, wearable devices, and augmented/virtual reality systems.


Key Parameters:
- **Full-scale Accelerometer Range:** $\pm 2g, \pm 4g, \pm 8g, \pm 16g$
- **Full-scale Gyroscope Range:** $\pm 125, \pm 245, \pm 500, \pm 1000, \pm 2000, \pm 4000$ dps
- **Supply Voltage (Vdd):** 1.71V to 3.6V
- **I/O Voltage (Vdd_IO):** 1.08V to Vdd + 0.1V
- **Interfaces:** $I^2C$, $I^3C$ (MIPI), SPI (3/4-wire)
- **Special Features:** Qvar detection, Sensor Hub, OIS/EIS dedicated outputs, and embedded Pedometer/Tilt/Significant Motion functions.
