Component ID: USB
Component Type: USB Type-C Receptacle Connector
Description:
This is a USB Type-C female receptacle connector designed for surface-mount (SMT) applications. It provides a complete USB 3.1/Type-C interface, including four high-speed differential pairs (TX/RX), two sideband use (SBU) lines, two configuration channel (CC) lines for power delivery and cable orientation detection, multiple VBUS and GND pins for power distribution, and a robust metal shell for shielding and mechanical retention.

Key Parameters:
- **Voltage Rating (AC)**: 20 VAC
- **Rated Current**: 5 A
- **Operating Temperature**: -40°C to +85°C
- **Initial Contact Resistance**: 40 mΩ Max.
- **Final Contact Resistance**: 50 mΩ Max.
- **Withstanding Voltage**: 100 VAC for 1 minute
- **Insulation Resistance**: 100 MΩ Min. @ 500 VDC
- **Insertion Force**: 5 N to 20 N (cycles 1-10,000)
- **Withdrawal Force**: 8 N to 20 N (cycles 1-1,000); 6 N to 20 N (cycles 1,001-10,000)
- **Mechanical Durability**: 10,000 mating cycles
