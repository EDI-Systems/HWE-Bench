# Features and Benefits

Low Turn-on Voltage Fast Switching PN Junction Guard Ring for Transient and ESD Protection  Totally Lead-Free & Fully RoHS Compliant (Notes 1 & 2) Halogen and Antimony Free. “Green” Device (Note 3) Qualified to AEC-Q101 Standards for High Reliability An Automotive-Compliant Part is Available Under Separate Datasheet (BAT54Q_AQ_CQ_SQ)

# Description

200mA surface mount Schottky Barrier Diode in SOT23 package, offers low turn-on voltage and fast switching capability, designed with PN Junction Guard Ring for Transient and ESD Protection, totally lead-free finish and RoHS compliant, ”Green” device.
