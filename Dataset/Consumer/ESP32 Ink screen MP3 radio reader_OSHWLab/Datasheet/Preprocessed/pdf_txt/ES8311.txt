Component ID: ES8311
Component Type: Low Power Mono Audio CODEC (Coder-Decoder)
Description: The ES8311 is a low-power, high-performance mono audio CODEC integrating a multibit delta-sigma ADC and DAC. It features a flexible I2S/PCM serial audio interface that can operate in master or slave mode, supports various system clocks (including 256/384Fs and USB clocks), and is configured via an I2C interface. It includes a microphone input path with a pre-amplifier, ALC, noise gate, and support for both analog and digital microphones, as well as a headphone driver output path with pop/click suppression. It is designed for portable and battery-powered audio applications.

Key Parameters:
- ADC Resolution & SNR: 24-bit, 100 dB SNR
- ADC THD+N: -93 dB
- DAC Resolution & SNR: 24-bit, 110 dB SNR
- DAC THD+N: -80 dB
- Sampling Frequency Range: 8 kHz to 96 kHz
- Supply Voltage Range: 1.8V to 3.3V
- Power Consumption (Playback & Record): 14 mW
- Audio Interface: I2S/PCM, Master/Slave selectable
- Control Interface: I2C (up to 400 kbps)
- Package: QFN-20
- Operating Temperature: -40°C to +105°C
