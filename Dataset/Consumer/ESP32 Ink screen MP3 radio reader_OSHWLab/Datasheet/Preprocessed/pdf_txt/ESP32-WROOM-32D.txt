Component ID:ESP32
Component Type:Wi-Fi + Bluetooth + BLE System-on-Chip (SoC) Module  
Description: 
The ESP32-WROOM-32D and ESP32-WROOM-32U are highly integrated, generic-purpose modules built around the ESP32-D0WD dual-core microcontroller. They provide comprehensive wireless connectivity including Wi-Fi (802.11 b/g/n), Bluetooth Classic, and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). The modules are designed for a wide range of applications from low-power sensor networks to demanding tasks like audio processing, featuring rich peripherals, configurable CPU clock speeds, and advanced power management with deep sleep currents below 5 µA. The primary difference between the D and U variants is the antenna interface: the 32D has an onboard PCB antenna, while the 32U integrates a U.FL connector for an external antenna.

Key Parameters:
- Core:Dual-core Xtensa® 32-bit LX6 microprocessor (ESP32-D0WD)
- CPU Clock Frequency:Adjustable from 80 MHz to 240 MHz
- Operating Voltage:3.0 V to 3.6 V
- Wi-Fi Output Power:Up to 20 dBm
- Wi-Fi Data Rate:Up to 150 Mbps
- Bluetooth:Bluetooth v4.2 BR/EDR and BLE specification
- Integrated Flash Memory:32 Mbit (4 MB) SPI flash
- Operating Temperature Range:–40 °C to +85 °C
- Sleep Current:< 5 µA
- Dimensions (WxLxH):32D: 18.00 mm × 25.50 mm × 3.10 mm; 32U: 18.00 mm × 19.20 mm × 3.20 mm
