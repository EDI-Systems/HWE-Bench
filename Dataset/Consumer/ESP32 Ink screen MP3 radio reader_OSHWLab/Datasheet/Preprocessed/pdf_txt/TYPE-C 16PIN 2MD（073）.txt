Component ID: TYPE-C
Component Type: USB Type-C Female Receptacle (SMT Connector)
Description:
This is a 16-pin surface-mount USB Type-C receptacle connector. It is designed for data and power transfer, supporting the USB Type-C standard. The specification details its mechanical durability, electrical performance (including contact resistance and insulation), and environmental reliability (temperature, humidity, soldering). It is rated for 5V/5A DC operation.

Key Parameters:
- Rated Voltage/Current: 5V DC, 5A
- Operating Temperature Range: -25°C to +85°C
- Contact Resistance (Initial): ≤ 10 mΩ (at ≤100mA)
- Insulation Resistance: ≥ 100 MΩ (at 100V DC)
- Dielectric Strength: Withstand 100V AC (50Hz) for 1 min
- Insertion Force: 0.5 - 2.0 kgf
- Withdrawal Force: 0.8 - 2.0 kgf
- Mechanical Life (Durability): 5000 mating cycles
- Solder Heat Resistance (Reflow): Peak temperature 260°C ±5°C for ≤20s
- Solder Heat Resistance (Hand): Iron temperature 330°C ±5°C for 3s ±0.5s
