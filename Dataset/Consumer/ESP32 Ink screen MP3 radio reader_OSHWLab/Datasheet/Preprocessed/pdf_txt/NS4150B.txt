Component ID: NS4150
Component Type: Mono Class-D Audio Power Amplifier  
Description: The NS4150B is a 3W mono, filterless Class-D audio power amplifier designed for low EMI and high efficiency in portable audio applications. It integrates a PWM modulation structure with built-in gain, eliminating the need for an external LC filter. The device features comprehensive protection circuits (over-current, over-temperature, and under-voltage), excellent PSRR, and low pop-and-click noise during power-on/off sequences. It operates from a single 3.0V to 5.0V supply and is offered in an MSOP-8 package.

Key Parameters:
- Supply Voltage Range (Recommended): 3.0V to 5.0V
- Output Power (THD=10%, VDD=5V, RL=4Ω): 2.8W
- Maximum Efficiency: 90%
- Quiescent Current (VDD=3.6V, No Load): 3.0 mA (typ)
- Modulation Frequency: 400 kHz (typ)
- PSRR (217 Hz): -80 dB (typ)
- THD+N (VDD=3.6V, Po=0.5W, RL=4Ω, 1kHz): 0.10% (typ)
- Shutdown Leakage Current (VDD=3.6V): 0.1 µA (typ)
- Operating Temperature Range: -40°C to 85°C
