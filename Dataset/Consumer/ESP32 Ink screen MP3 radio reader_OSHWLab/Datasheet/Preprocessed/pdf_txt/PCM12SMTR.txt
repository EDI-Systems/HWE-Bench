Component ID: PCM12
Component Type: Ultraminiature Surface Mount Slide Switch
Description:
The PCM Series comprises ultraminiature, low-profile, surface-mount slide switches available in SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) and SP3T (Single Pole Triple Throw) configurations. They function as manual electromechanical selectors, connecting a common terminal to one of several output terminals depending on the actuator's position. Designed for automated assembly, they are RoHS compliant and packaged on tape and reel for high-volume applications in computer peripherals, telecommunications, and test equipment.

Key Parameters:
- Contact Rating (Max Switching Current/Voltage): 0.3A @ 6 VDC
- Mechanical Life: 10,000 cycles
- Initial Contact Resistance: 70 mΩ max.
- Insulation Resistance: 100 MΩ min. @ 500V
- Dielectric Strength: 500 VAC min.
- Operating Temperature Range: –10°C to +85°C
- Max Solder Temperature: 255°C
- Switch Function (SPDT Model): ON1-2 (Position 1), ON2-3 (Position 2)
- Switch Function (SP3T Model): ON1-3 (Position 1), ON2-3 (Position 2), ON3-4 (Position 3)
