Component ID: ME6210
Component Type: Low-Dropout (LDO) Linear Voltage Regulator
Description:
The ME6210 series are CMOS-based, low-quiescent-current linear voltage regulators designed for high-voltage input applications up to 18V. They provide a fixed, accurate output voltage with a typical output current capability of 500mA. Key features include very low dropout voltage, ultra-low ground current, built-in short-circuit protection, and stability with ceramic capacitors, making them suitable for battery-powered and portable electronic devices.

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Input Voltage (VIN): 18 V
- Output Voltage Range (VOUT): 1.5V to 5.0V (Fixed variants: 2.5V, 2.8V, 3.0V, 3.3V, 3.6V, 4.0V, 5.0V)
- Maximum Output Current (IOUT): 500 mA (Typ., Vin=4V, Vout=3V)
- Dropout Voltage (VDIF): 11 mV (Typ. @ Iout=10mA, Vout=3.0V)
- Quiescent (Supply) Current (ISS): 1.5 µA (Typ.)
- Output Voltage Accuracy: ±2%
- Line Regulation: 0.03 %/V (Typ.)
- Short-Circuit Current: 50 mA (Typ.)
- Operating Temperature Range (Topr): -25°C to +85°C
