Component ID: SHT
Component Type: Digital Humidity and Temperature Sensor
Description:
The SHT3x-DIS is a fully calibrated, linearized, and temperature-compensated digital sensor for measuring relative humidity and ambient temperature. It features a CMOSens® chip with an integrated I2C interface supporting speeds up to 1 MHz and two user-selectable addresses. The sensor provides high accuracy and fast measurement times, operates from a wide supply voltage range, and is housed in a miniature DFN package.

Key Parameters:
- Supply Voltage Range: 2.15 V to 5.5 V
- I2C Communication Speed: Up to 1 MHz
- Humidity Accuracy (Typical, SHT35): ±1.5 %RH
- Temperature Accuracy (Typical, SHT35, 20-60°C): ±0.1 °C
- Humidity Measurement Range: 0 to 100 %RH
- Temperature Measurement Range: -40 to 125 °C
- Humidity Resolution: 0.01 %RH
- Temperature Resolution: 0.01 °C
- Package Type: 8-Pin DFN
- Package Dimensions: 2.5 x 2.5 x 0.9 mm
