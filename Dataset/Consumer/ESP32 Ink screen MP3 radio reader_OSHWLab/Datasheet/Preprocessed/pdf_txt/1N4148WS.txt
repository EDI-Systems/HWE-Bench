Component ID: 1N4148WS
Component Type: Fast Switching Diode
Description: The 1N4148WS is a surface-mount, general-purpose, fast-switching silicon diode. It is characterized by a very fast reverse recovery time (4 ns), making it suitable for high-speed switching applications, clipping, clamping, and rectification in circuits where switching speed is critical. It comes in a small SOD-323 package.

Key Parameters:
- Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 75 V
- Average Rectified Forward Current (IO): 200 mA
- Forward Voltage (VF): 0.715 V (typ @ 1 mA) to 1.25 V (max @ 150 mA)
- Reverse Recovery Time (trr): 4 ns
- Junction Capacitance (CJ): 2.0 pF (max @ VR=0V, f=1 MHz)
- Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 1.0 µA (max @ VR=75V), 25 nA (max @ VR=20V)
- Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 2.0 A (@ t=1.0 µs), 1.0 A (@ t=1.0 s)
- Power Dissipation (PD): 200 mW
- Operating Temperature Range (Tj, TSTG): -65°C to +150°C
