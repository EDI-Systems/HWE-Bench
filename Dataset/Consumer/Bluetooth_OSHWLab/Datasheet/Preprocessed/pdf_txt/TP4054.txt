Component ID: TP4054
Component Type: Single-Cell Lithium-Ion Battery Linear Charger IC
Description:
The TP4054 is a complete monolithic constant-current/constant-voltage linear charger for single-cell lithium-ion batteries. It integrates a power MOSFET and thermal regulation, requiring no external blocking diode or sense resistor. Designed for USB power specifications, it features a preset 4.2V charge voltage with ±1% accuracy, programmable charge current up to 500mA, and automatic charge termination when the current drops to 1/10 of the set value. It includes battery reverse-connection protection, under-voltage lockout (UVLO), automatic recharge, and status indication.

Key Parameters:
- **Charging Algorithm**: Constant-Current (CC) / Constant-Voltage (CV)
- **Input Voltage Range (VCC)**: 4.25V to 6.5V (Operating), Absolute Max: Vss-0.3V to Vss+7V
- **Programmable Charge Current (IBAT)**: Up to 500mA (typ), set by external resistor (RPROG)
- **Final Float (Charge) Voltage**: 4.2V (±1%)
- **Charge Termination Threshold**: 1/10 of the programmed charge current
- **Trickle Charge Threshold Voltage**: 2.9V
- **Thermal Regulation Temperature**: ~120°C
- **Standby Supply Current**: 25µA (when disabled)
- **Package**: SOT-23-5L
- **BAT Pin Absolute Max Voltage**: Vss-0.3V to 7V
