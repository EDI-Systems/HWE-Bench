Component ID: SK-32
Component Type: Slide Switch
Description:
This component is a single-pole, double-throw (SPDT) slide switch with a momentary action mechanism. It functions by sliding the actuator to make or break an electrical connection between its common terminal and one of two output terminals, returning to a default position when released. It is designed for manual circuit control in electronic devices.

Key Parameters:
- Rated Voltage/Current: 250V AC / 1mA
- Insulation Resistance: 100 MΩ min (at DC 250V)
- Contact Resistance: 100 mΩ max (at DC 100mV)
- Operating Temperature Range: -25°C to +70°C
- Mechanical Life: 10,000 cycles
- Operating Force: 250 ± 100 gf
- Terminal Length (L) Options: 1.5 mm, 2.0 mm, 2.5 mm
