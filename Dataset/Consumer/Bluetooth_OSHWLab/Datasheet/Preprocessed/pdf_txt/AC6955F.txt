Component ID: AC6955F4
Component Type: Bluetooth Audio System-on-Chip (SoC) with integrated DSP, Audio Codec, and PMU
Description:
The AC6955F4 is a highly integrated Bluetooth audio SoC designed for stereo headsets. It features a 240MHz 32-bit DSP with FPU for audio processing, a Bluetooth 5.1 BR/EDR/BLE radio, a multi-format audio decoder (including SBC, AAC, MP3, FLAC), and a complete audio codec with DAC, ADC, and microphone interfaces. It integrates a full-speed USB OTG controller and a rich set of peripherals (UART, SPI, I2C, PWM, SD host) into a QSOP24 package, targeting low-power portable audio applications.

Key Parameters:
- **Core Processor**: 32-bit DSP with Hardware FPU
- **Core Clock Speed**: Up to 240 MHz
- **Bluetooth Version**: 5.1 (BR/EDR + BLE)
- **RF Transmit Power**: +6 dBm
- **RF Receiver Sensitivity**: -90 dBm
- **Audio DAC SNR**: ≥ 95 dB (16-bit, 2-channel)
- **Audio ADC SNR**: ≥ 90 dB (16-bit, 3-channel)
- **Supply Voltage (VDDIO)**: 2.2V to 3.6V
- **Low-Power Current**: 3 µA (Soft-off mode)
- **Operating Temperature**: -20°C to +70°C
