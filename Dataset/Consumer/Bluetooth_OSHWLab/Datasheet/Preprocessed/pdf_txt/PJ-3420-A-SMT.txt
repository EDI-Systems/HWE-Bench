Component ID: PJ-3420
Component Type: 3.5mm Stereo Audio Jack (SMT)
Description: The PJ-3420-A-SMT is a surface-mount 3.5mm stereo phone jack used for audio input or output in compact electronic devices. It features a standard Tip-Ring-Sleeve (TRS) configuration and includes integrated shunt switches for both the Tip and Ring contacts. These switches are mechanically normally closed and are disconnected when a plug is inserted, making them ideal for "plug detection" or automatic audio routing (e.g., muting internal speakers when headphones are connected).

Key Parameters:
- Connector Size: 3.5mm
- Mounting Style: Surface Mount (SMT)
- Number of Conductors: 3 (Stereo)
- Internal Switches: 2 (Tip Switch, Ring Switch)
