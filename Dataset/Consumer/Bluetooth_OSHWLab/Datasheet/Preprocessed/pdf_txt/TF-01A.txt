Component ID: TF-01A
Component Type: MicroSD (TF) Card Connector
Description:
This component is a surface-mount, push-push type MicroSD (TransFlash) card connector. Its primary functionality is to provide a mechanical interface and electrical connection for a removable MicroSD memory card, facilitating data transfer and storage expansion in portable electronic devices. The connector features a card detection switch and a write-protect detection switch, and it is designed for automated assembly with tape and reel packaging.

Key Parameters:
- Card Type: MicroSD (TF) Card
- Contact Resistance: ≤ 50 mΩ
- Insulation Resistance: ≥ 100 MΩ
- Withstanding Voltage: 500 V AC for 1 minute
- Operating Temperature: -25°C to +85°C
- Mechanical Life (Insertion/Withdrawal): 10,000 cycles
- Actuation Force (Card Ejection): 4.9 N ~ 14.7 N (500gf ~ 1500gf)
