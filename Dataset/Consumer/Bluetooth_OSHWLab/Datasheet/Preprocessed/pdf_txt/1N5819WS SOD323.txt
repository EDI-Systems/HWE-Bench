Component ID: 1N5819WS
Component Type: Schottky Barrier Diode
Description:
This component is a Schottky barrier diode series featuring a low forward voltage drop, guard ring construction for transient protection, negligible reverse recovery time, and low reverse capacitance. It is designed for high-efficiency switching applications where fast switching and low power loss are critical.

Key Parameters:
- Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 40V (1N5819WS), 30V (1N5819BWS), 20V (1N5819CWS)
- Forward Continuous Current (IF(AV)): 350 mA
- Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 1.5 A (pulse ≤1.0s)
- Forward Voltage (VF): Max 0.60 V (at IF=200mA)
- Reverse Current (IR): Max 5.0 μA (at rated VR)
- Junction Capacitance (CT): Typ 50 pF (VR=0V, f=1MHz)
- Reverse Recovery Time (trr): Typ 10 ns
- Power Dissipation (Pd): 400 mW
- Thermal Resistance (RθJA): 300 °C/W
- Package: SOD-323
