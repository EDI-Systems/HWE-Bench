Component ID: TC8871
Component Type: Mono Audio Power Amplifier (Class AB/D Selectable)
Description:
The TC8871 is a single-channel, 5W audio power amplifier designed for portable and low-voltage audio systems. It features selectable Class AB or Class D operation to balance efficiency and audio quality, with built-in feedback resistors that simplify external circuitry by eliminating the need for output coupling capacitors, bootstrap capacitors, or snubber networks. Operating from a 2.0V to 5.5V supply, it offers high power output (e.g., 5W into 2Ω at 5V), includes a shutdown mode for low power consumption, and integrates thermal shutdown protection. Its design minimizes FM interference and is suitable for applications like USB-powered speakers, laptops, and multimedia audio systems.

Key Parameters:
- Supply Voltage Range (Recommended): 2.0 V to 5.5 V
- Maximum Output Power (THD+N <10%, 1 kHz): 5 W (into 2Ω load at 5V)
- Quiescent Supply Current (Static): 6 mA (Typical)
- Shutdown Leakage Current: 0.8 μA (Typical)
- Power Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR): 80 dB (Typical)
- Total Harmonic Distortion + Noise (THD+N): 0.2% (Typical, Aᵥ=2, Pₒ=0.5W, 4Ω)
- Gain Bandwidth Product: 2.5 MHz
- Operating Temperature Range: -40 °C to 85 °C
- Thermal Resistance (θⱼₐ, ESOP-8): 140 °C/W
