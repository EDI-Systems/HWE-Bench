Component ID: MCP73831T
Component Type: Single-Cell Lithium-Ion Battery Linear Charger IC
Description:
The MCP73831T-2ATI/OT is a fully integrated constant-current/constant-voltage linear charger for single-cell lithium-ion batteries. Designed for USB power compliance, it requires minimal external components. It features programmable charge current up to 500mA, thermal regulation, automatic charge termination (C/10), and a preset 4.2V charge voltage. It includes battery reverse protection at the output, a charge status indicator, automatic recharge, and low-power shutdown/standby modes.

Key Parameters:
- **Charge Voltage (Preset)**: 4.2V (Typical, 4.158V Min, 4.242V Max)
- **Programmable Charge Current**: Up to 500mA (Typical, set via external resistor RPROG)
- **Input Voltage Range (Operating)**: 4.25V to 6.0V
- **Maximum Power Dissipation (PDMAX)**: 250 mW
- **Standby Current (Charge Terminated)**: 200 µA (Typical)
- **Shutdown Current**: 25 µA (Typical)
- **Trickle Charge Threshold Voltage (VTRKL)**: 2.9V (Typical)
- **BAT Pin Reverse Leakage Current (Standby)**: -2.5 µA (Typical)
- **Operating Ambient Temperature Range**: -40°C to +85°C
