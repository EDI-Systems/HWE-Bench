Component ID: RDA5807
Component Type: Single-Chip Broadcast FM Stereo Radio Tuner IC  
Description:  
The RDA5807MP is a fully integrated CMOS FM tuner that receives FM stereo broadcasts from 50 MHz to 115 MHz. It incorporates a digital low‑IF receiver with synthesizer, IF selectivity, RDS/RBDS decoder, MPX decoder, and stereo audio DACs, requiring minimal external components. It is controlled via an I²C interface and includes an integrated LDO regulator, making it suitable for portable devices.

Key Parameters:
- Frequency Range: 50 MHz to 115 MHz
- Supply Voltage (VDD): 2.7 V to 3.3 V
- Total Current Consumption: < 20 mA (typical at 3.0 V)
- Channel Spacing: 25 kHz, 50 kHz, 100 kHz, 200 kHz
- Reference Clock Support: 32.768 kHz, 12 MHz, 13  MHz, 19.2 MHz, 24 MHz, 26 MHz, 38.4 MHz
- Audio Output Load: Directly drives 32 Ω load
- Control Interface: I²C (2‑wire bus)
- Package: SOP‑8
