Component ID: USB
Component Type: USB 3.1 Type-C Receptacle Connector (6-Pin, SMT)
Description:
This is a surface-mount USB Type-C receptacle connector compliant with the USB 3.1 standard. It is a 6-pin version, providing the essential power (VBUS), ground (GND), and configuration channel (CC) lines required for basic USB Type-C power delivery and device detection, but does not include the high-speed SuperSpeed data lanes (TX/RX). Its primary functionality is to serve as a compact, durable, and electrically robust physical and electrical interface for connecting USB Type-C cables, supporting power delivery up to 100W (20V/5A) and data communication via USB 2.0 signals multiplexed over the CC pins.

Key Parameters:
- Current Rating (VBUS Pin): 5 A
- Current Rating (GND Pin): 1.25 A
- Current Rating (Other Pins): 0.25 A
- Rated Voltage: 20 V DC Max
- Withstanding Voltage: 100 V AC RMS
- Contact Resistance: 40 mΩ Max
- Insulation Resistance: 100 MΩ Min
- Operating Temperature: -25°C to +85°C
- Mechanical Durability: 10,000 mating cycles
- Insertion Force: 5 ~ 20 N
- Extraction Force: 8 ~ 20 N
