Component ID: MK12C02
Component Type: Tactile Push Button Switch (Surface Mount)  
Description:  
This is a miniature, surface-mount, momentary tactile push button switch designed for low-voltage DC applications. It provides a normally-open (NO) contact that closes when the actuator is pressed, with a specified mechanical life and electrical ratings suitable for consumer electronics, control panels, and PC board interfaces.

Key Parameters:
- Switch Type: Momentary, Normally Open (NO)
- Rated Electrical Load: 12V DC at 0.5A / 6V DC at 1.0A
- Contact Resistance: ≤100 mΩ (max)
- Insulation Resistance: ≥100 MΩ at 100V DC
- Withstand Voltage: 250V AC for 1 minute
- Operating Temperature: -20°C to +70°C
- Mechanical Life: 10,000 cycles (min)
- Actuator Height (Switch Height): 1.5 mm (G015), 2.0 mm (G020), or 2.5 mm (G025)
- Reflow Soldering Temperature: ≤250°C (max)
