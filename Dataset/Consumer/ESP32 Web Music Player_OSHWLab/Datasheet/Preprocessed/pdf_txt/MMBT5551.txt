Component ID: MMBT5551
Component Type: NPN Bipolar Junction Transistor (BJT)
Description: The MMBT5551 is a general-purpose NPN bipolar junction transistor in a SOT-23 surface-mount package. It is designed for medium-power amplification and switching applications, featuring epitaxial planar construction and high voltage ratings.

Key Parameters:
- Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 180 V (Max)
- Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 160 V (Max)
- Collector Current (Ic): 0.6 A (Max, DC)
- Collector Power Dissipation (Pc): 0.35 W (Max)
- DC Current Gain (hFE): 30 to 300 (at Vce=5V, depending on Ic)
- Transition Frequency (fT): 100 to 300 MHz
- Collector-Emitter Saturation Voltage (VCE(sat)): 0.15 V (Max at Ic=10mA, Ib=1mA)
- Base-Emitter Saturation Voltage (VBE(sat)): 1 V (Max at Ic=10mA, Ib=1mA)
- Thermal Resistance, Junction to Ambient (RθJA): 357 °C/W
