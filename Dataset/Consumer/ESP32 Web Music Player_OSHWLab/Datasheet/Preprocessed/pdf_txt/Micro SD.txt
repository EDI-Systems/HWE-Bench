Component ID: Micro sd
Component Type: Memory Card Socket (SD / microSD)
Description: This is a family of surface-mount card sockets designed for SD and microSD cards, compliant with UHS-I and UHS-II interfaces. They provide a physical and electrical interface for memory cards in portable and embedded devices, featuring mechanisms like push-push or push-pull for card insertion/ejection, shielding for EMI/ESD protection, and a space-saving, low-profile design suitable for double-sided PCB assembly.

Key Parameters:
- Card Type Compatibility: SD, microSD (UHS-I, UHS-II)
- Number of Positions (Contacts): 8 (microSD), 9 (SD)
- Mechanism Type: Push-Push, Push-Pull, or Hinge
- Socket Height: Ranges from 1.45mm to 2.70mm (ultra-thin)
- Current Rating: 0.5A per contact
- Voltage Rating: 5V AC
- Contact Resistance: 100mΩ max.
- Insertion/Extraction Force: 14N max.
- Mechanical Durability: 10,000 cycles
- Operating Temperature Range: -55°C to +85°C (environmental test condition)
