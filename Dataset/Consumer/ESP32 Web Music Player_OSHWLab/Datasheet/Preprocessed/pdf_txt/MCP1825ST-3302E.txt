Component ID: MCP1825
Component Type: Low Dropout (LDO) Linear Voltage Regulator
Description:
The MCP1825/MCP1825S is a family of 500 mA low-dropout linear regulators designed for applications requiring high current at low output voltages. It features a low quiescent current, stable operation with small ceramic output capacitors, and is available in both fixed-output (MCP1825S: 3-pin; MCP1825: 5-pin) and adjustable-output (MCP1825: 5-pin) versions. Key protections include short-circuit current limiting and overtemperature shutdown. The MCP1825 variant includes a shutdown (SHDN) pin for ultra-low standby current and a power-good (PWRGD) output signal (fixed version) or an adjust (ADJ) pin (adjustable version).

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Output Current: 500 mA
- Input Voltage Range: 2.1 V to 6.0 V
- Dropout Voltage: 210 mV (Typical at 500 mA load)
- Output Voltage Range (Adjustable): 0.8 V to 5.0 V
- Output Voltage Tolerance: 0.5% (Typical)
- Quiescent (Supply) Current: 120 µA (Typical)
- Shutdown Current: 0.1 µA (Typical, MCP1825 only)
- Minimum Output Capacitance: 1.0 µF (Ceramic, for stability)
- Power Good Delay: 110 µs (Typical, fixed-output MCP1825)
- Operating Junction Temperature: -40°C to +125°C
