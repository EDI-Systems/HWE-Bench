Component ID: USB
Component Type: Micro-USB 2.0 Connector (Receptacle & Plug)
Description:
The ZX Series is a family of extremely small, high-reliability connectors compliant with the physical, electrical, and environmental requirements of the Micro-USB standard (USB 2.0). It includes both receptacles and plugs in various mounting styles (SMT, through-hole, mid-mount, reverse mount) and interface configurations (Micro-A, Micro-B, Micro-AB), enabling high-speed data transfer up to 480 Mbps and supporting applications like data transmission, charging, and audio. A unique contact and lock design ensures high durability and a positive mating tactile sensation.

Key Parameters:
- **Data Transfer Rate**: 480 Mbps (USB 2.0 Hi-Speed)
- **Current Rating (Per Contact)**: 1 A
- **Current Rating (Contacts 1 & 5)**: 1.8 A
- **Current Rating (Contacts 2, 3, 4)**: 0.5 A
- **Voltage Rating**: 30 VAC
- **Contact Resistance**: 30 mΩ max. (at 100mA)
- **Withstanding Voltage**: 100 VAC for 1 minute (no flashover/breakdown)
- **Operating Temperature**: -30°C to +85°C (includes temperature rise from current)
- **Durability**: 10,000 mating cycles (contact resistance rise ≤ 10 mΩ from initial)
- **Insertion/Removal Force**: Insertion ≤ 35 N max., Removal ≥ 8 N min.
