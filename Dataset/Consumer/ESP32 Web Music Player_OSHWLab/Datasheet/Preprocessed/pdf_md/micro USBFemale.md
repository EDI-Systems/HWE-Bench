# ■Overview

Extremely small high reliability connectors complying with physical, electrical and environmental requirements of Micro-USB standard (USB 2.0).

Receptacles and plugs are available in a wide variety of mounting and termination configurations, permitting their use in various applications.

# ■Features

# 1. Size reduction

Compared with the standard Mini-B connectors, the size of ZX connectors is reduced by approximately 60% while still allowing a high-speed data transfer of 480 Mbps, specified in USB 2.0.

# 2. Receptacle styles

Two interface configurations: Micro-B and Micro-AB in standard, mid-mount and reverse mounting styles. SMT and through-hole (shell) PC board terminations

# 3. Plug styles

Corresponding to the receptacle styles, two interface configurations: Micro-B and Micro-A, with direct wire or PCB wire soldering. Several plug styles can be used for data transmission, earphone or charging applications.

# 4. High durability

Unique contact and lock configuration assures high durability while maintaining easy insertion / disconnection. Fully mated condition (when mated with corresponding HRS connector) is confirmed by a definite tactile sensation
