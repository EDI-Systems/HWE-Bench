Component ID: 78L05
Component Type: Positive Fixed-Voltage Linear Regulator (LDO)
Description:
The 78LXX series are monolithic integrated circuit voltage regulators designed to provide a stable, fixed positive output voltage from a higher, unregulated input voltage. They are suitable for low-power applications requiring up to 100mA of supply current and include built-in protection features such as thermal overload protection and short-circuit current limiting.

Key Parameters:
- **Output Voltage Options (Fixed)**: 5V, 6V, 8V, 9V, 10V, 12V, 15V, 24V
- **Maximum Output Current**: 100 mA
- **Maximum Input Voltage (Vi)**: 30 V (for Vo=5V,8V); 35 V (for Vo=12V,15V)
- **Dropout Voltage (Typical)**: 1.7 V (for 78L05, Tj=25°C)
- **Line Regulation (Max)**: 150 mV (7V≤Vi≤20V, for 78L05)
- **Load Regulation (Max)**: 60 mV (Io=1mA~100mA, for 78L05)
- **Quiescent Current (Max)**: 5.5 mA
- **Ripple Rejection (Min)**: 40 dB (f=120Hz, for 78L05)
- **Operating Junction Temperature**: -20°C to +120°C
- **Maximum Power Dissipation**: 700 mW
