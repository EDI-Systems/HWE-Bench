Component ID: SLT038125T220MUB
Component Type: Through-Hole (Axial Lead) Toroidal (Magnetic Ring) Inductor
Description:
This is a vertical, through-hole mounted toroidal inductor designed for power filtering and energy storage in electronic circuits. It consists of a magnetic core (T038125) wound with insulated copper wire, providing a nominal inductance of 22 µH. Its primary function is to oppose changes in current, commonly used in DC-DC converters, power supply input/output filtering, and noise suppression applications.

Key Parameters:
- **Inductance (L)**: 22 µH ±20% (Tested at 1kHz, 0.25V)
- **DC Resistance (DCR)**: 32 mΩ MAX
- **Rated RMS Current (Irms)**: 3.0 A (Defined by temperature rise ΔT ≤ 40°C)
- **Core Material / Type**: 125 (Material Code), T038125 (Toroid Size)
- **Winding Specifications**: 16.5 turns of Φ0.60mm 2UEW-F insulated copper wire
- **Operating Temperature Range**: Verified from -25°C to +100°C (Reliability Tests)
- **Lead Diameter (D)**: 0.60 mm ±0.05
- **Body Height (C)**: 10.0 mm ±2.0
- **Body Diameter / Width (A)**: 12.5 mm MAX
- **Body Thickness / Depth (B)**: 6.5 mm MAX
