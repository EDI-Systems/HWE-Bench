Component ID: SG3525A
Component Type: Pulse Width Modulator (PWM) Controller IC
Description:
The SG3525A series are integrated circuits designed for controlling switching power supplies. They generate a variable-duty-cycle pulse train (PWM) to regulate output voltage/current. Key features include an internal 5.1V trimmed reference, an error amplifier with a common-mode range that includes the reference, a wide-frequency oscillator (sync-capable), adjustable dead time control, internal soft-start, undervoltage lockout with hysteresis, a pulse-terminating latch to prevent multiple pulses per cycle, and dual totem-pole output drivers capable of sourcing and sinking current.

Key Parameters:
- Supply Voltage (Operating): 8 V to 35 V
- Reference Output Voltage: 5.1 V (trimmed to ±1% for SG2525A, ±2% for SG3525A)
- Oscillator Frequency Range: 100 Hz to 500 kHz
- Output Driver Current (Source/Sink): > 200 mA (Typical), 500 mA (Absolute Max)
- Undervoltage Lockout Hysteresis: ~500 mV
- Dead Time Control: Adjustable via external resistor
- Soft-Start: Controlled via external capacitor
- Total Power Dissipation (Max): 1000 mW
- Operating Temperature Range (SG3525A): 0°C to 70°C
