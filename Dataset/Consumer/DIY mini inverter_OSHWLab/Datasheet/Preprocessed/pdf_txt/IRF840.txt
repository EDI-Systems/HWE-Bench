Component ID: IRF840
Component Type: N-Channel Power MOSFET
Description: The IRF840 is a high-voltage, N-channel enhancement mode power MOSFET designed for high-speed switching applications. It features low on-resistance and a high breakdown voltage, making it suitable for off-line switch-mode power supplies (SMPS), DC-to-DC converters, and motor controllers. The device includes an integrated body diode and is typically housed in a TO-220 package, which allows for efficient heat dissipation when mounted to a heatsink.
