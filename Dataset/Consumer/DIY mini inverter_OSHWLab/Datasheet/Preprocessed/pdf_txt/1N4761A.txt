Component ID: 1N4761
Component Type: Silicon Planar Power Zener Diode
Description:
This is a family of silicon planar power Zener diodes designed for voltage stabilization and clipping circuits in high-power applications. They operate in the reverse breakdown (Zener) region to maintain a nearly constant voltage across their terminals despite variations in current or supply voltage, and can also conduct in the forward direction like a standard diode. The series offers a wide range of nominal Zener voltages from 3.0V to 100V, packaged in a DO-41 glass case.

Key Parameters:
- **Nominal Zener Voltage (Vz nom)**: 3.0V to 100V (specific per part number, e.g., 1N4727A = 3.0V, 1N4732A = 4.7V)
- **Zener Voltage Range (Vz)**: Specific tolerance range per part number (e.g., 1N4727A: 2.85V - 3.15V)
- **Maximum Power Dissipation (Ptot)**: 1.0 W (at Ta=25°C with leads at ambient 8mm from case)
- **Maximum Zener Impedance (Zzt)**: Ranges from 10 Ω (e.g., 3.0V) to 900 Ω (e.g., 100V) at the test current Izt
- **Maximum Regulator Current (Izm)**: Ranges from 275 mA (3.0V) to 12.5 mA (100V) at Ta=25°C
- **Maximum Surge Current (Izm (surge))**: Ranges from 1375 mA (3.0V) to 62 mA (100V) at Ta=25°C
- **Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IR)**: Ranges from 150 μA (3.0V) to 10 μA (100V) at specified test voltage VR
- **Junction Temperature (Tj)**: 200 °C (maximum)
- **Forward Voltage (VF)**: 1.2 V (maximum at IF = 200 mA)
