Component ID: MR30PB-F
Component Type: Board-mount, 3-pin, molded power connector (wire-to-board/board-to-board)
Description:
The MR30PB-F is a vertical, board-mount, 3-pin power connector designed for space-constrained PCBs, particularly for integration with motors in lithium battery-powered applications. It provides a wire-to-board or board-to-board connection solution, aiming to eliminate separate wiring by allowing direct integration between the motor and controller.

Key Parameters:
- Rated Current: 20A MAX (ΔT<85°C)
- Withstanding Voltage: 500V DC
- Insulation Resistance: ≥2000 MΩ
- Contact Resistance: ≤1.2 mΩ
- Mechanical Life (Mating Cycles): 100 cycles
- Operating Temperature: -20°C to 120°C
- Ingress Protection (IP) Rating: IP40
- Flammability Rating: UL94 V-0
- Housing Material: PA (Polyamide/Nylon), colors: Yellow, Black, Blue
- Contact Material: Copper alloy, plating: Gold
