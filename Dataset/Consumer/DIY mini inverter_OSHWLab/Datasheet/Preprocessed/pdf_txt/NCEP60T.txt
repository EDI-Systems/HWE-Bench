Component ID: NCEP60T12A
Component Type: N-Channel Power MOSFET
Description: The NCEP60T12A is an N-channel enhancement mode power MOSFET that utilizes Super Trench technology to provide excellent $R_{DS(on)}$ and low gate charge. This device is designed for high-efficiency power management and switching applications, such as DC-DC converters, motor control, and synchronous rectification. Its high current handling capability and robust design make it suitable for demanding industrial and consumer electronics.

Key Parameters:
- **Drain-to-Source Voltage ($V_{DSS}$):** 60V
- **Continuous Drain Current ($I_D$):** 120A (at $T_C = 25^\circ\text{C}$)
- **Static Drain-to-Source On-Resistance ($R_{DS(on)}$):** 4.2 m$\Omega$ (typ), 5.0 m$\Omega$ (max)
- **Gate-to-Source Voltage ($V_{GS}$):** $\pm 20$V
- **Total Gate Charge ($Q_g$):** 95nC (typ)
- **Power Dissipation ($P_D$):** 170W (at $T_C = 25^\circ\text{C}$)
- **Operating Junction Temperature:** -55 to +175°C
- **Package:** TO-220 / TO-263
