Component ID: AVR ISP
Component Type: In-Circuit Serial Programming (ICSP) Interface
Description: The AVR ISP 6-pin header (also known as the ICSP header) is a standardized 2x3 male pin header interface used for In-System Programming of Atmel (now Microchip) AVR microcontrollers. It provides a direct connection to the Serial Peripheral Interface (SPI) pins and the Reset line, allowing developers to upload firmware or burn bootloaders to the microcontroller without removing it from the target PCB. This specific 6-pin configuration is the compact industry standard found on most Arduino-compatible boards.

Key Parameters:
- **Connector Type:** 2x3 Male Pin Header
- **Pitch:** 2.54 mm (0.1 inch)
- **Interface Protocol:** SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface)
- **Required Signals:** MISO, SCK, MOSI, RESET, VCC, GND
- **Operating Voltage:** 3.3V or 5V (System dependent)
- **Durability:** Standard gold-plated pins for reliable mating
