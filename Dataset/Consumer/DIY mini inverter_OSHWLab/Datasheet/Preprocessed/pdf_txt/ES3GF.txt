Component ID: ES3GF
Component Type: Super Fast Recovery Rectifier
Description: The ES3GF is a surface-mount, super-fast recovery power rectifier. It is designed for applications requiring high efficiency and rapid switching, such as high-frequency inverters, freewheeling diodes, and polarity protection. It features a glass-passivated chip junction for enhanced reliability and a low forward voltage drop to minimize power loss. The "Super Fast" characteristic ensures a very short reverse recovery time, reducing switching losses in power supply circuits.

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage: 400V
- Maximum Average Forward Current: 3.0A
- Reverse Recovery Time ($t_{rr}$): 35ns
- Maximum Forward Voltage: 1.3V (at 3.0A)
- Peak Forward Surge Current: 100A
- Operating Junction Temperature: -55 to +150 Celsius
- Package: SMB (DO-214AA)
