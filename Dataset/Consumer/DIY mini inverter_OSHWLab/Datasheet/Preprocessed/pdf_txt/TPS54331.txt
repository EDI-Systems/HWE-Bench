Component ID: TPS54331  
Component Type: Non-synchronous Step-Down (Buck) DC-DC Converter with Integrated High-Side MOSFET  
Description:  
The TPS54331 is a monolithic buck converter designed to deliver up to 3 A of continuous output current from an input voltage range of 3.5 V to 28 V. It integrates an 80 mΩ high-side MOSFET and employs current-mode control with internal slope compensation, enabling stable operation with ceramic output capacitors. Key features include a fixed 570 kHz switching frequency, pulse-skipping Eco-mode™ for high efficiency at light loads, adjustable output voltage down to 0.8 V, programmable undervoltage lockout (UVLO), and comprehensive protection functions such as cycle-by-cycle current limit, frequency foldback, overvoltage transient protection, and thermal shutdown. It is offered in 8-pin SOIC and thermally enhanced SOIC PowerPAD packages.

Key Parameters:
- Input Voltage Range: 3.5 V to 28 V
- Continuous Output Current: Up to 3 A
- High-Side MOSFET On-Resistance (RDS(on)): 80 mΩ (typical)
- Switching Frequency: Fixed 570 kHz (typical)
- Output Voltage Range: Adjustable down to 0.8 V
- Shutdown Quiescent Current: 1 μA (typical)
- Operating Junction Temperature Range: -40°C to 150°C
- Package Options: SOIC-8 (4.90 mm × 3.90 mm) and SO PowerPAD-8
