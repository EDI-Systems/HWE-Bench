Component ID: RDA5807MP
Component Type: Single-Chip Broadcast FM Radio Tuner
Description: The RDA5807MP is a highly integrated, single-chip broadcast FM stereo radio tuner. It features a fully integrated synthesizer, IF selectivity, RDS/RBDS, and an MPX decoder. Supporting worldwide frequency bands from 50 MHz to 115 MHz, it requires minimal external components and is designed to directly drive a 32-ohm headphone load. Its low power consumption and high integration make it a standard choice for portable devices like mobile phones, media players, and DIY radio modules.

Key Parameters:
- Frequency Range: 50 MHz to 115 MHz (Worldwide support)
- Operating Voltage: 2.7V to 3.3V
- Interface: $I^2C$ (Serial Data and Clock)
- Audio Output: Stereo headphone drive (32-ohm load)
- Features: RDS/RBDS Support, Bass Boost, Soft Mute
