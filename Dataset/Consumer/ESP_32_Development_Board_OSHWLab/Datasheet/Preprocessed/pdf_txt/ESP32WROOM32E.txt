Component ID: ESP32
Component Type: Wi-Fi + Bluetooth + BLE MCU Module
Description: The ESP32-WROOM-32E-N8 is a powerful, generic Wi-Fi+BT+BLE MCU module based on the ESP32-D0WD-V3 chip. It is designed for a wide range of applications, from low-power sensor networks to demanding tasks like voice encoding and music streaming. The module features a dual-core processor, integrated antenna, and extensive peripherals. The "N8" suffix specifically indicates that this version is equipped with 8MB of onboard SPI flash memory.

Key Parameters:
- Microcontroller: ESP32-D0WD-V3 (Dual-core Xtensa® 32-bit LX6)
- Clock Frequency: Adjustable from 80 MHz to 240 MHz
- Flash Memory: 8 MB
- Operating Voltage: 3.0V to 3.6V (3.3V Typical)
- Connectivity: 802.11 b/g/n Wi-Fi and Bluetooth V4.2 BR/EDR & BLE
