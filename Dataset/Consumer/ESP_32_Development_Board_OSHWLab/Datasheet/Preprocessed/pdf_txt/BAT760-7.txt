Component ID: BAT760
Component Type: Surface Mount Schottky Barrier Rectifier Diode
Description: The BAT760-7 is a Schottky diode designed for high-efficiency rectification, featuring a very low forward voltage drop and high conductance. It is constructed with a guard ring for transient protection and is qualified for high-reliability applications, including automotive (AEC-Q101). It is a lead-free, halogen-free, and RoHS-compliant "Green" device in a SOD323 package.

Key Parameters:
- Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 30 V
- Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 1 A
- Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 5.5 A (8.3ms)
- Forward Voltage Drop (VF): 270 mV (Typical at IF = 10mA), 350 mV (Typical at IF = 100mA), 550 mV (Max at IF = 1A)
- Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 10 µA (Max at VR = 5V), 20 µA (Max at VR = 8V), 50 µA (Max at VR = 15V)
- Total Capacitance (CT): 25 pF (Typical at f = 1MHz, VR = 5V)
- Power Dissipation (PD): 235 mW
- Thermal Resistance, Junction to Ambient (RθJA): 426 °C/W
- Operating Junction Temperature Range (TJ): -65 to +150 °C
