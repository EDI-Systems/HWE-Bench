Component ID: LSM6DSMTR
Component Type: iNEMO Inertial Module (3D Accelerometer and 3D Gyroscope)
Description: The LSM6DSMTR is a high-performance 6-axis inertial measurement unit (IMU) that integrates a 3D digital accelerometer and a 3D digital gyroscope. It is designed for "always-on" high-accuracy applications such as smartphones, optical image stabilization (OIS), and IoT devices. The module features ultra-low power consumption, an embedded sensor hub for collecting data from external sensors, and specialized features like a pedometer, tilt detection, and significant motion detection.

Key Parameters:
- Supply Voltage (VDD): 1.71V to 3.6V
- Measurement Range (Accel): $\pm 2g$, $\pm 4g$, $\pm 8g$, $\pm 16g$
- Measurement Range (Gyro): $\pm 125$, $\pm 245$, $\pm 500$, $\pm 1000$, $\pm 2000$ dps
- Interfaces: $I^2C$, $I^3C$ (MIPI), and SPI (Standard/Auxiliary)
