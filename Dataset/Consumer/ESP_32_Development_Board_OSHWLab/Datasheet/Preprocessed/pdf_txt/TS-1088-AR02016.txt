Component ID: SW
Component Type: Tactile Switch / Push Button Switch
Description:
This component is a surface-mount, low-profile tactile switch designed for PCB mounting. It functions as a momentary, normally-open (NO) push-button switch, where pressing the button completes an electrical circuit between its two terminals. It features a metal dome (弹片) for tactile feedback, a dust-proof membrane, and is rated for low-current signal or power applications.

Key Parameters:
- Rated Voltage & Current: 12V DC, 50mA
- Contact Resistance: ≤ 100 mΩ
- Operating Force: 160gf, 260gf, or 50gf ±50gf (depending on variant)
- Travel: 0.2 ± 0.1 mm
- Insulation Resistance: ≥ 100 MΩ
- Dielectric Strength: ≥ 250 VAC for 1 minute
- Actuator Height (H): 1.8mm, 2.0mm, or 2.5mm (variant)
- Terminal Type: Surface Mount (SMD)
