Component ID: SS8050-G
Component Type: NPN Bipolar Junction Transistor (BJT)
Description:
The SS8050-G is an NPN bipolar junction transistor in a SOT-23 surface-mount package, designed for general-purpose amplification and switching applications. It functions as a current-controlled device where a small base current controls a larger collector current, enabling signal amplification, driving loads, or acting as an electronic switch. It features a high DC current gain and a transition frequency suitable for low to medium frequency circuits.

Key Parameters:
- Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 25 V (Max)
- Collector Current (Ic): 1.5 A (Max)
- Collector Power Dissipation (Pc): 300 mW (Max)
- DC Current Gain (hFE): 200 to 350 (at VCE=1V, Ic=100mA)
- Collector-Emitter Saturation Voltage (VCE(sat)): 0.5 V (Max, at Ic=800mA, IB=80mA)
- Transition Frequency (fT): 100 MHz (Min)
- Thermal Resistance (RθJA): 417 °C/W
- Junction Temperature (Tj): 150 °C (Max)
