Component ID: 2N7002
Component Type: N-Channel Enhancement Mode Small Signal MOSFET
Description: This is a 60V, 115 mA N-channel MOSFET designed for low-power switching and amplification applications. It features a low threshold voltage and is housed in a SOT-23 package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs in automotive and general-purpose circuits.

Key Parameters:
- Drain-Source Voltage (VDSS): 60 V
- Continuous Drain Current (ID): 115 mA @ 25°C
- Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V (Continuous)
- Drain-Source On-State Resistance (RDS(on)): 7.5 Ω max @ VGS = 10 V, ID = 500 mA
- Gate Threshold Voltage (VGS(th)): 1.0 to 2.5 V
- Total Power Dissipation (PD): 225 mW (FR-4 board, TA=25°C)
- Input Capacitance (Ciss): 50 pF typical
- Forward Transconductance (gFS): 80 mS typical
- Junction Temperature Range (TJ, Tstg): -55 to +150 °C
