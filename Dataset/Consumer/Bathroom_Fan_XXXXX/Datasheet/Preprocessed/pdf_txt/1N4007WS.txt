Component ID: 1N4007
Component Type: SOD-323 Surface Mount Diode
Description: This is a general-purpose, fast-switching silicon diode in a SOD-323 surface-mount package. It is characterized by high conductance and a fast reverse recovery time of 4 ns, making it suitable for high-speed switching applications. Key electrical ratings include a maximum repetitive peak reverse voltage of 300V, an average rectified forward current of 300mA, and a low junction capacitance of 2pF.
