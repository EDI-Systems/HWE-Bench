Component ID: HB611-6.35
Component Type: PCB Terminal Block / Connector
Description: This is a 6.35mm pitch, screw-terminal PCB connector block designed for wire-to-board connections. It features a UL 94V-0 rated PA66 insulation body and tin-plated copper alloy contacts. It is rated for 20-24A current and 300-450V voltage, supporting wire sizes from AWG 22-12 or 2.5mm² conductor cross-section. The component is designed for through-hole mounting on a PCB with a recommended hole diameter of 1.5mm.
