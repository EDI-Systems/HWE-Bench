Component ID: 3401
Component Type: P-Channel Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET
Description:
The 3401 is a P-Channel logic-level enhancement mode power field-effect transistor (FET) manufactured using high cell density, advanced trench technology to achieve an extremely low on-state resistance (RDS(ON)). It is designed for low-voltage applications requiring minimal power loss and high current capability in a compact SOT23-3L surface-mount package. Its primary function is to act as a high-efficiency electronic switch for power management, enabling or disabling power to a load in applications such as load switches, DC/DC converters, and portable equipment.

Key Parameters:
- Drain-Source Voltage (VDSS): -30 V (Absolute Maximum)
- Gate-Source Voltage (VGSS): ±12 V (Absolute Maximum)
- Continuous Drain Current (ID): -4.3 A (Typ. @ VGS=-10V, Tc=25°C)
- Drain-Source On-Resistance (RDS(ON)): 44 mΩ (Typ. @ VGS=-10V, ID=-4.3A)
- Gate Threshold Voltage (VGS(th)): -0.6 V to -1.2 V
- Total Gate Charge (Qg): 6.2 nC (Typ.)
- Input Capacitance (Ciss): 672 pF (Typ. @ VGS=0V, f=1MHz)
- Power Dissipation (PD): 1.5 W (TA=25°C)
- Thermal Resistance (RθJA): 120 °C/W
- Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): 150 °C (Max)
