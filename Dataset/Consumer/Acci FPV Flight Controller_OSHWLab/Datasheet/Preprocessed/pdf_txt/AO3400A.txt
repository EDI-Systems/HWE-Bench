Component ID: AO3400A
Component Type: N-Channel Enhancement Mode MOSFET
Description:
The AO3400A is an N-channel enhancement mode power MOSFET utilizing advanced trench technology and a low-resistance SOT-23 package to achieve an extremely low on-state resistance (Rds(on)). It is designed for applications such as load switching and PWM control, offering high current handling in a compact form factor.

Key Parameters:
- **Drain-Source Voltage (Vds)**: 30 V (Maximum)
- **Continuous Drain Current (Id)**: 5.7 A (Ta=25°C)
- **Static Drain-Source On-Resistance (Rds(on))**: 18 mΩ (Typ, Vgs=10V, Id=5.7A)
- **Gate Threshold Voltage (Vgs(th))**: 0.65 V to 1.45 V (Min to Max)
- **Gate-Source Voltage (Vgs)**: ±12 V (Maximum)
- **Total Gate Charge (Qg)**: 8.5 nC (Typ, Vgs=4.5V)
- **Input Capacitance (Ciss)**: 630 pF (Typ, Vds=15V, Vgs=0V)
- **Power Dissipation (Pd)**: 1.4 W (Max, Ta=25°C)
- **Junction-to-Ambient Thermal Resistance (RθJA)**: 90 °C/W (Max, t ≤ 10s)
