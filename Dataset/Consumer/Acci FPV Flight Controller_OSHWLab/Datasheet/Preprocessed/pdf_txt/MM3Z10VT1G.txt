Component ID: MM3Z10VT1G
Component Type: Zener Diode
Description: The MM3Z10VT1G is a surface-mount Zener diode in a compact SOD-323 package. It is designed for voltage regulation and protection in applications where board space is at a premium. Unlike standard diodes, a Zener diode is specifically designed to operate in the reverse-bias breakdown region, allowing it to maintain a stable reference voltage despite variations in current. This makes it ideal for voltage clamping, regulation, and overvoltage protection.


Key Parameters:
- **Nominal Zener Voltage ($V_Z$):** 10V
- **Power Dissipation ($P_D$):** 300 mW
- **Zener Impedance ($Z_{ZT}$):** 20 $\Omega$ (at $I_{ZT} = 5\text{mA}$)
- **Operating Junction Temperature:** -65 to +150°C
- **Package:** SOD-323
