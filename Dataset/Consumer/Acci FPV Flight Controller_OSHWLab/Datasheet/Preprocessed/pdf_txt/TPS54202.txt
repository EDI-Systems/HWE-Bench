Component ID: TPS54202  
Component Type: Synchronous Step-Down (Buck) DC/DC Converter  
Description:  
The TPS54202 is a monolithic synchronous buck converter with a wide 4.5 V to 28 V input range, capable of delivering up to 2 A of continuous output current. It integrates both high-side and low-side MOSFETs, employs peak current-mode control with internal loop compensation, and operates at a fixed 500 kHz switching frequency. Key features include a 5 ms internal soft-start, frequency spread spectrum for reduced EMI, Advanced Eco‑mode for high light‑load efficiency, and comprehensive protection (cycle‑by‑cycle current limit, hiccup overcurrent, overvoltage, and thermal shutdown), all housed in a compact 6‑pin SOT‑23 package.

Key Parameters:
- Input Voltage Range: 4.5 V to 28 V (operating)
- Maximum Output Current: 2 A (continuous)
- Switching Frequency: 500 kHz (fixed)
- High‑Side MOSFET On‑Resistance: 148 mΩ
- Low‑Side MOSFET On‑Resistance: 78 mΩ
- Quiescent Current: 45 μA (typical)
- Shutdown Current: 2 μA (typical)
- Soft‑Start Time: 5 ms (internal)
- Operating Temperature Range: –40 °C to 125 °C (junction)
