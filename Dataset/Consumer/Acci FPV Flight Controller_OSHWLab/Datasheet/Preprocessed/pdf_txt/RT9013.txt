Component ID: TPRT9013
Component Type: Low-Dropout (LDO) Linear Voltage Regulator
Description:
The TPRT9013 is a high-performance, 500mA low-dropout linear voltage regulator designed for space-constrained portable and RF/wireless applications. It features an extremely high power supply rejection ratio (PSRR) and ultra-low dropout voltage, ensuring stable output voltage even with a small difference between input and output. The device includes an enable (CE) pin for shutdown control to minimize power consumption and is available in fixed output voltage variants within a compact SOT23-5 package.

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Output Current: 500 mA (Typical)
- Dropout Voltage: 100 mV (Typical at 100mA load), 300 mV (Typical at 200mA load)
- Quiescent (Supply) Current: 60 µA (Typical)
- Power Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR): 70 dB (Typical at 1 kHz)
- Output Voltage Accuracy: ±2% tolerance
- Input Voltage Range: Up to 7.0 V (Maximum)
- Shutdown Current: <1 µA (Typical)
- Package / Power Dissipation: SOT23-5, 250 mW
