Component ID: MPU-6000
Component Type: 6-Axis Motion Tracking Device (3-Axis Gyroscope + 3-Axis Accelerometer) with Digital Motion Processor (DMP)
Description: The MPU-60X0 is a fully integrated 6-axis Motion Processing Unit that combines a 3-axis MEMS gyroscope and a 3-axis MEMS accelerometer on a single chip, along with an onboard Digital Motion Processor (DMP) capable of running complex 9-axis sensor fusion algorithms. It features an auxiliary I2C master bus to interface with external sensors (like a magnetometer) and communicates with a host processor via either I2C (MPU-6050 & MPU-6000) or SPI (MPU-6000 only). Its primary function is to provide precise motion sensing and processing for applications like gaming, navigation, and gesture recognition.

Key Parameters:
- Gyroscope Full-Scale Range: Programmable to ±250, ±500, ±1000, ±2000 °/sec (dps)
- Accelerometer Full-Scale Range: Programmable to ±2g, ±4g, ±8g, ±16g
- Digital Motion Processor (DMP): Onboard processor for sensor fusion algorithms
- Communication Interfaces: I2C (up to 400 kHz) for MPU-6050; I2C & SPI (up to 20 MHz) for MPU-6000
- Supply Voltage (VDD): Logic and analog supply for MPU-6000
- Logic Level Reference (VLOGIC): Pin to define I2C interface voltage levels for MPU-6050
- Auxiliary I2C Master: For connecting and managing external sensors
- Package: 4x4x0.9 mm QFN (Quad Flat No-leads)
- Operating Temperature: Not specified in provided text, but typical for MEMS sensors (-40°C to +85°C)
