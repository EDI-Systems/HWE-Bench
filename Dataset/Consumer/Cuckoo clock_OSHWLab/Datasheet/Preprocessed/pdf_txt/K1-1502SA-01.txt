Component ID: TP4057ST26P
Component Type: Linear Li-Ion Battery Charger (Single Cell)
Description: The TP4057 is a complete constant-current/constant-voltage (CC/CV) linear charger for single-cell lithium-ion batteries. Its SOT-23-6 package and low external component count make it ideal for portable applications. No external sense resistor or blocking diode is required. It features a preset 4.2V charge voltage with 1% accuracy and programmable charge current up to 500mA. The device includes two open-drain status indicator pins (CHRG# and STDBY#) to signify charging activity and full charge completion.

Key Parameters:
- Input Supply Voltage (VCC): 4.0V to 9.0V
- Maximum Charge Current: 500mA (Programmable)
- Regulated Output Voltage: 4.2V (+/- 1%)
- Trickle Charge Threshold: 2.9V
- Standby Current: 40uA (typ)
- Thermal Protection: Internal regulation for temperature control
- Package: SOT-23-6
