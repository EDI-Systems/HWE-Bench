Component ID: SS34LH
Component Type: Surface Mount Schottky Barrier Rectifier Diode
Description:
The SS32LH through SS320LH are a series of 3A surface-mount Schottky barrier rectifier diodes designed for high-efficiency power conversion applications. They feature a low forward voltage drop, ultra-high-speed switching capability, and a low-profile DO-214AC (SMA) package to save board space. These diodes are suitable for use as rectifiers, freewheeling diodes, and polarity protection in circuits requiring high current capability and fast recovery.

Key Parameters:
- **Max Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM)**: 20V to 200V (depending on part number)
- **Max Average Forward Rectified Current (IO)**: 3.0 A
- **Max Forward Voltage Drop (VF)**: 0.40V to 0.85V @ 3A, TA=25°C (depending on part number)
- **Max Reverse Current (IR)**: 0.5 mA @ VRRM, TA=25°C / 20 mA @ VRRM, TA=100°C
- **Max Forward Surge Current (IFSM)**: 125 A (8.3ms single half sine-wave)
- **Junction-to-Ambient Thermal Resistance (RθJA)**: 55 °C/W (Typical)
- **Operating Junction Temperature (TJ)**: -55°C to +150°C (SS32LH-SS310LH) / -55°C to +175°C (SS315LH-SS320LH)
- **Diode Junction Capacitance (Cj)**: 250 pF (Typical) @ f=1MHz, VR=4V
