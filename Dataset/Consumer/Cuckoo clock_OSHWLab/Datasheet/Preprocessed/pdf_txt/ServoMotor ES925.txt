Component ID: ES9251II
Component Type: Micro Servo Motor (RC Servo)
Description:
The ES9251II is a lightweight, low-profile micro servo motor designed for radio-controlled models. It utilizes a PWM control system to convert pulse width signals into precise angular position control of its output shaft. It features a 360° continuous rotation capability (though standard operating travel is limited), a gear train with one metal and five plastic gears, and is characterized by its low operating current and compact size.

Key Parameters:
- Operating Voltage Range: 4.8V to 6.0V (specified)
- No-Load Operating Speed: 0.09 sec/60° @ 4.8V, 0.08 sec/60° @ 6.0V
- Stall Torque: 0.25 kg·cm @ 4.8V, 0.3 kg·cm @ 6.0V
- Maximum (Stall) Current: 120 mA @ 4.8V, 140 mA @ 6.0V
- Idle Current: 2.2 mA
- Operating Temperature Range: -10°C to 60°C
- Weight: 3.0 ± 0.5 g
- Control Signal: PWM, 700-2300 µs pulse width range
- Standard Operating Travel: 90° ± 10° (for 1000-2000 µs pulse)
- Gear Type: 1 metal gear + 5 plastic gears
