Component ID: OLED
Component Type: OLED Display Module (Graphic Display Module)
Description:
This is a 1.54-inch monochrome OLED graphic display module with a resolution of 128x64 pixels, driven by the SSD1309 controller. Its core functionality is to provide a visual output interface by controlling individual pixels to form images or text. It supports configurable communication interfaces, including 4-wire SPI and I²C, allowing flexible integration with various microcontrollers. The module features a wide operating voltage (3.3V to 5V), low power consumption, a high viewing angle (>160°), and is available in white-on-black or blue-on-black color variants.

Key Parameters:
- Display Size: 1.54 inch
- Resolution: 128 x 64 pixels
- Display Technology: OLED (Monochrome)
- Active Area: 35.052 mm x 17.516 mm
- Interface: Configurable 4-wire SPI or I²C
- Operating Voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Operating Temperature: -20°C to 60°C
- Viewing Angle: >160°
- Driver IC: SSD1309
- Module Dimensions: 42.40 mm x 38.00 mm
