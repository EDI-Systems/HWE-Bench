Component ID: IRLM
Component Type: P-Channel Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET
Description:
This is a P-Channel MOSFET utilizing advanced trench technology, designed for efficient power switching in a compact SOT-23 package. Its primary function is to act as a voltage-controlled switch, connecting or disconnecting a load from a power source. Key features include a low on-state resistance (Rds(on)) and low gate charge, enabling high-efficiency switching for applications such as load/power switching, interfacing, and logic level shifting. It is characterized by a -20V drain-source voltage rating and a continuous drain current of -4.1A.

Key Parameters:
- Drain-Source Voltage (VDSS): -20 V
- Continuous Drain Current (ID): -4.1 A (Tc=25°C)
- Drain-Source On-State Resistance (Rds(on)): 52 mΩ max @ Vgs=-4.5V, Id=-4.1A
- Gate Threshold Voltage (Vgs(th)): -0.45V to -1.0V (Id=-250µA)
- Total Gate Charge (Qg): 7.8 nC (Typ)
- Input Capacitance (Ciss): 740 pF (Typ)
- Maximum Power Dissipation (PD): 1.7 W
- Gate-Source Voltage (Vgs): ±12 V (Max)
