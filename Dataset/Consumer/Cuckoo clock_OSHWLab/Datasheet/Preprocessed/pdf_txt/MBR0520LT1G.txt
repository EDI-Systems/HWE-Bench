Component ID: MBR0520LT1G
Component Type: Surface Mount Schottky Power Rectifier
Description: The MBR0520LT1G is a Schottky barrier rectifier designed for use in low-voltage, high-frequency rectification, freewheeling, and polarity protection applications. It is housed in the compact SOD-123 surface-mount package. The device features a very low forward voltage drop and extremely fast switching speeds, which significantly reduces power dissipation and improves efficiency in portable and battery-powered electronics.

Key Parameters:
- Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage: 20V
- Average Rectified Forward Current: 0.5A
- Peak Forward Surge Current: 5.5A
- Maximum Forward Voltage: 0.385V (at 0.5A, 25 Celsius)
- Maximum Reverse Current: 250uA (at 20V, 25 Celsius)
- Operating Junction Temperature: -65 to +125 Celsius
- Package: SOD-123
