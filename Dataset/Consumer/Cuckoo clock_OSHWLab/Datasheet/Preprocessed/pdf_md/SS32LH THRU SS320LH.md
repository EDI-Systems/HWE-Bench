# SS32LH THRU SS320LH

3A Surface Mount Schottky Barrier Rectifiers

# ■ Features

Electrostatic discharge (ESD) test under IEC6100-4-2•   
standard >16ΚV(SS32AL\~SS36AL). standard >10ΚV(SS310AL\~SS320AL).   
Low profile surface mounted application in order to• optimize board space.   
Low power loss, high efficiency.•   
High current capability, low forward voltage drop.•   
Ultra high-speed switching.•   
Silicon epitaxial planar chip, metal silicon junction. Suffix • "H" indicates Halogen-free part, ex.SS32ALH. Lead  
free parts meet environmental standards of MIL  
STD-19500 /228

■ Mechanical data

• Epoxy:UL94-V0 rated flame retardant • Case : Molded plastic, DO-214AC / SMA • Terminals : Solder plated, solderable per MIL-STD-750, Method 2026 • Polarity : Indicated by cathode band • Weight : 0.002 ounce, 0.055 gram
