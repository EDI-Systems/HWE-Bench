Component ID: BMI160  
Component Type: Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU)  
Description: The BMI160 is a low-power, 6-axis inertial measurement unit that combines a 16-bit digital triaxial accelerometer and a 16-bit digital triaxial gyroscope in a single package. It is designed for precise motion sensing in battery-powered devices like smartphones, wearables, and IoT products. Key features include hardware-synchronized sensor data, an integrated FIFO buffer, a power management unit for advanced low-power operation, and support for external sensor data (e.g., magnetometers). It enables motion-based applications such as step counting, gesture recognition, image stabilization, and augmented reality.

Key Parameters:
- Power Supply Voltage (VDD): 1.71 V to 3.6 V
- I/O Voltage (VDDIO): 1.2 V to 3.6 V
- Current Consumption (Typical): 925 µA (accelerometer and gyroscope both active)
- Operating Temperature Range: Not explicitly stated in excerpt; refer to full datasheet.
- Accelerometer Resolution: 16-bit digital
- Gyroscope Resolution: 16-bit digital
- FIFO Buffer Size: 1024 bytes
- Package Dimensions: 2.5 x 3.0 x 0.83 mm (LGA)
- Interface Options: I²C or SPI (primary), secondary I²C for external sensors
- Interrupt Features: Any-motion, no-motion, step detection, significant motion, tap/double-tap, orientation, free-fall
