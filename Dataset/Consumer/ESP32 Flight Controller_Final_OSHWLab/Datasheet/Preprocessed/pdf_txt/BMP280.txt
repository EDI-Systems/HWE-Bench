Component ID: BMP280
Component Type: Digital Barometric Pressure Sensor (MEMS)
Description:
The BMP280 is an absolute barometric pressure and temperature sensor from Bosch Sensortec, designed for mobile and battery-powered applications. It utilizes piezo-resistive MEMS technology for high accuracy and linearity. The sensor provides calibrated digital output via I²C or SPI interfaces, features ultra-low power consumption, and includes configurable filtering and multiple measurement modes (sleep, forced, normal) for power-optimized operation. Its primary function is to enable altitude tracking, indoor/outdoor navigation, weather monitoring, and vertical velocity detection.

Key Parameters:
- Pressure Measurement Range: 300 ... 1100 hPa
- Relative Accuracy (Pressure): ±0.12 hPa (typical, 700–900 hPa @ 25°C)
- Absolute Accuracy (Pressure): ±1 hPa (typical, 950–1050 hPa, 0–40°C)
- Temperature Coefficient Offset (TCO): 1.5 Pa/K (equiv. to 12.6 cm/K)
- Current Consumption: 2.7 μA @ 1 Hz sampling rate (typical)
- Operating Temperature Range: -40 ... +85 °C
- Supply Voltage (VDD): Minimum 1.71 V
- Digital Interfaces: I²C (up to 3.4 MHz), SPI (3-wire & 4-wire, up to 10 MHz)
- Package: 8-pin LGA, 2.0 × 2.5 mm², height 0.95 mm
- Pressure Resolution: 0.16 Pa
