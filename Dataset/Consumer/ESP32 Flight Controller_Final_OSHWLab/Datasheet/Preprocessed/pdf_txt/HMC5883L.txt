Component ID: HMC5883L
Component Type: 3-Axis Digital Magnetometer / Compass IC
Description:
The HMC5883L is a low-power, surface-mount, multi-chip module integrating three anisotropic magnetoresistive (AMR) sensors and a dedicated ASIC. It provides digital 3-axis magnetic field sensing for applications like electronic compassing. The ASIC handles signal amplification, automatic degaussing (set/reset), offset cancellation, and includes a 12-bit ADC. Data is output via an I²C serial interface, enabling heading accuracy of 1° to 2°. It is designed for low-voltage operation in consumer and navigation devices.

Key Parameters:
- Supply Voltage (VDD): 2.16 V to 3.6 V
- I/O Voltage (VDDIO): 1.71 V to VDD+0.1 V
- Average Current (Measurement Mode): 100 µA (Typical, 7.5 Hz ODR, Single Supply)
- Magnetic Field Range: ±8 Gauss (Full Scale)
- Dynamic Range (Gain Control): ±1 Gauss to ±8 Gauss
- Digital Resolution: 12-bit ADC
- Output Data Rate (ODR): 0.75 Hz to 160 Hz
- Sensitivity (LSb/Gauss): 230 to 1370 (Programmable Gain)
- Noise Floor (Field Resolution): 2 milli-gauss (Typical)
- Package: 3.0 x 3.0 x 0.9 mm, 16-pin Leadless Chip Carrier (LCC)
