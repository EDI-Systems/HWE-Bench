Component ID: MPU-9250
Component Type: 9-Axis Motion Tracking Unit (IMU + Magnetometer + DMP)
Description:
The MPU-9250 is a multi-chip module (MCM) that integrates a 3-axis MEMS gyroscope, a 3-axis MEMS accelerometer, and a 3-axis magnetometer (AK8963) along with a Digital Motion Processor (DMP). It provides complete 9-axis sensor fusion output. The device digitizes all sensor outputs with 16-bit ADCs, includes programmable filters and full-scale ranges, and communicates via I2C or SPI interfaces. It is designed for motion tracking in portable and wearable electronics.

Key Parameters:
- Gyroscope Full-Scale Range: Programmable ±250, ±500, ±1000, ±2000 °/sec
- Accelerometer Full-Scale Range: Programmable ±2g, ±4g, ±8g, ±16g
- Magnetometer Full-Scale Range: ±4800 µT
- Operating Voltage (VDD): 2.4V to 3.6V
- Digital I/O Voltage (VDDIO): 1.71V to VDD
- Communication Interfaces: I2C (up to 400 kHz), SPI (1 MHz standard, up to 20 MHz for sensor/read interrupt)
- Package: 3x3x1 mm QFN
- Gyroscope Operating Current: 3.2 mA
- Sleep Mode Current: 8 µA
