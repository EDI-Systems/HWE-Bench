Component ID: BMP280
Component Type: Digital Barometric Pressure Sensor (MEMS)
Description:
The BMP280 is an absolute barometric pressure and temperature sensor from Bosch Sensortec, designed for mobile and battery-powered applications. It utilizes piezo-resistive MEMS technology for high accuracy, linearity, and long-term stability. The sensor provides compensated digital pressure and temperature data via I²C or SPI interfaces, featuring ultra-low power consumption, multiple operating modes (sleep, forced, normal), and an integrated IIR filter for noise reduction. Its primary applications include altitude tracking, indoor/outdoor navigation, weather monitoring, and vertical velocity indication.

Key Parameters:
- Pressure Measurement Range: 300 to 1100 hPa
- Relative Accuracy (Pressure): ±0.12 hPa (700–900 hPa @ 25°C)
- Absolute Accuracy (Pressure): Typ. ±1 hPa (950–1050 hPa, 0 to +40°C)
- Temperature Coefficient Offset (TCO): 1.5 Pa/K (equiv. to 12.6 cm/K)
- Current Consumption: 2.7 µA @ 1 Hz sampling rate (typical)
- Supply Voltage (VDD): Minimum 1.71 V
- Digital Interfaces: I²C (up to 3.4 MHz), SPI (3- and 4-wire, up to 10 MHz)
- Operating Temperature Range: -40 to +85 °C
- Package: 8-pin LGA, 2.0 x 2.5 mm², 0.95 mm height
