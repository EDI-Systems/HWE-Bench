Component ID: MPU-6050
Component Type: 6-Axis Motion Tracking Device (Gyroscope + Accelerometer)
Description: The MPU-6050 is a highly integrated 6-axis motion tracking device that combines a 3-axis gyroscope and a 3-axis accelerometer on a single silicon die. It features an onboard Digital Motion Processor (DMP) that processes complex 6-axis MotionFusion algorithms, reducing the load on the host microcontroller. Additionally, it includes a dedicated auxiliary I2C bus to interface with external sensors such as magnetometers, allowing for a complete 9-axis output. It is widely used in applications requiring orientation and motion sensing, such as drones, smartphones, and wearable devices.

Key Parameters:
- Gyroscope Range: ±250, ±500, ±1000, ±2000 °/s (Programmable)
- Accelerometer Range: ±2g, ±4g, ±8g, ±16g (Programmable)
- Interface: I2C (Up to 400kHz)
- Operating Voltage (VDD): 2.375V to 3.46V
