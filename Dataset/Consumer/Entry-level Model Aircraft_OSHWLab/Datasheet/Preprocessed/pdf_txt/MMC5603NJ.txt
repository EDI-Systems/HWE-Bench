Component ID: MMC5603NJ  
Component Type: Monolithic 3-Axis AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance) Magnetic Sensor with Digital Interface  

Description:  
The MMC5603NJ is a complete, monolithic 3-axis magnetic sensor that integrates AMR sensing elements, signal processing, and a digital I²C/I3C interface. It measures magnetic fields with a full-scale range of ±30 Gauss, offering high resolution (down to 0.0625 mG/LSB) and low noise (2 mG RMS). Key features include an on-chip automatic degaussing (SET/RESET) function to eliminate temperature-induced offset and residual magnetization, built-in sensitivity compensation, a temperature sensor, and low power consumption. It is designed for applications such as electronic compassing and position sensing, requiring minimal external components.

Key Parameters:
- Field Range (Full Scale): ±30 G
- Resolution: 0.0625 mG per LSB (20-bit operation mode)
- Total RMS Noise: 2 mG (typical)
- Supply Voltage (VDD): 1.62 V to 3.6 V
- Supply Current: 0.75 mA to 4.0 mA (depending on bandwidth mode)
- Power-Down Current: 1.0 µA (typical)
- Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
- Output Data Rate: Up to 1000 Hz (sensor frequency response)
- Interface: I²C (Fast Mode, ≤400 kHz) and I3C
- Package Size: 0.8 mm × 0.8 mm × 0.4 mm (Wafer Level Package)
