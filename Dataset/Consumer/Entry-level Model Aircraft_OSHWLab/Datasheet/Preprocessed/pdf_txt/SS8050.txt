Component ID: SS8050
Component Type: NPN Bipolar Junction Transistor (BJT)
Description:
The S 8050S is a general-purpose NPN bipolar junction transistor housed in an SOT-23 surface-mount package. It is designed for amplification and switching applications, capable of handling collector currents up to 1.5A. Its key functionality is to provide current gain, where a small base current controls a larger collector current. It is specified as the complementary pair to the PNP SS8550 transistor.

Key Parameters:
- Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 25 V (Max)
- Collector Current (Ic): 1.5 A (Max Continuous)
- Collector Power Dissipation (Pc): 300 mW (Max)
- DC Current Gain (hFE): 120 to 400 (Ranked L, H, J; VCE=1V, Ic=100mA)
- Collector-Emitter Saturation Voltage (VCE(sat)): 0.5 V (Max; Ic=800mA, IB=80mA)
- Transition Frequency (fT): 100 MHz (Min; VCE=10V, Ic=50mA)
- Thermal Resistance, Junction-to-Ambient (RthJA): 417 °C/W
- Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 5 V (Max)
