Component ID: MK1
Component Type: Wi-Fi Microcontroller Module
Description: This is an ESP8266-based Wi-Fi module, specifically following the ESP-12 series footprint. It integrates a Tensilica L106 32-bit RISC microprocessor, Flash memory, and a Wi-Fi transceiver into a compact surface-mount package. It is a staple in IoT (Internet of Things) development due to its low cost, integrated networking stack, and support for environments like Arduino and MicroPython. The module requires specific strapping pin configurations (IO0, IO2, IO15) during power-up to select between running from Flash or entering the UART bootloader.

Key Parameters:
- Processor: Tensilica L106 32-bit MCU (80MHz / 160MHz)
- Wi-Fi: 802.11 b/g/n (2.4 GHz)
- Operating Voltage: 3.0V to 3.6V (3.3V Recommended)
- Flash Memory: Typically 4MB (32Mbit)
