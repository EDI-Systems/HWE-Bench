Component ID: SM4410
Component Type: N-Channel Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET
Description: The SM4410PRL is an N-channel logic-level power MOSFET designed for high-density applications. It utilizes advanced trench technology to provide extremely low on-resistance ($R_{DS(on)}$) and superior switching performance. This component is typically housed in an SO-8 package and is widely used in power management, DC-DC converters, and load switching for portable devices and consumer electronics.

Key Parameters:
- Drain-Source Voltage ($V_{DS}$): 30V
- Continuous Drain Current ($I_D$): ~10A
- Static Drain-Source On-Resistance ($R_{DS(on)}$): ~13.5mΩ @ $V_{GS}$ = 10V
- Gate-Source Voltage ($V_{GS}$): ±20V
