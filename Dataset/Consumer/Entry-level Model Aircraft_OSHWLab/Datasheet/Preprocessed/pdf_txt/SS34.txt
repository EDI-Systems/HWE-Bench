Component ID: SS34
Component Type: Schottky Barrier Rectifier
Description: The SS34 is a surface-mount Power Schottky Rectifier designed for use in low-voltage, high-frequency inverters, free-wheeling, and polarity protection applications. It is highly efficient due to its low forward voltage drop and fast switching characteristics. The device is typically encapsulated in a DO-214AC (SMA) or DO-214AB (SMC) package and features a guard ring for overvoltage protection.

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage ($V_{RRM}$): 40V
- Maximum Average Forward Rectified Current ($I_{F(AV)}$): 3.0A
- Maximum Forward Voltage ($V_F$): 0.5V @ 3.0A
- Peak Forward Surge Current ($I_{FSM}$): 100A
