Component ID: IP5306
Component Type: Integrated Power Management SOC (System-on-Chip) for Power Banks
Description:
The IP5306 is a highly integrated power management SOC that combines a synchronous boost converter, lithium-ion battery charging management, and battery level indication into a single chip, providing a complete power solution for mobile power banks. It features synchronous switching for both charging (up to 2.1A) and discharging/boosting (up to 2.4A), supports simultaneous charging and discharging via built-in power path management, and drives 1-4 LEDs for battery level display. It includes a button control for power-on and flashlight driving, automatic load detection for entering low-power standby, and requires only a single external inductor.

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Boost/Discharge Output Current: 2.4 A
- Maximum Charging Input Current: 2.1 A
- Boost Converter Efficiency: Up to 92%
- Charging Efficiency: Up to 91%
- Standby Current: < 100 µA
- Battery Charge Termination Voltage Options: 4.20 V / 4.30 V / 4.35 V / 4.40 V
- Boost Output Voltage: 5 V
- Charging Input Voltage: 5 V (DC)
- Protection Features: Output OVP, OCP, SCP; Input OVP, Battery OVP, OCP, OTP; ESD 4KV
- Package: ESOP8
