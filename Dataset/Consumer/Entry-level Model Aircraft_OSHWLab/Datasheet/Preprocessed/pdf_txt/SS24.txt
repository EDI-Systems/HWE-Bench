Component ID: SS24
Component Type: Schottky Barrier Rectifier
Description: The SS24 is a surface-mount Schottky barrier diode designed for use in low-voltage, high-frequency inverters, free-wheeling, and polarity protection applications. It is characterized by an extremely low forward voltage drop and very fast switching speeds, which minimize power loss and improve efficiency in high-speed power conversion circuits.

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage ($V_{RRM}$): 40V
- Maximum Average Forward Rectified Current ($I_{F(AV)}$): 2.0A
- Maximum Forward Voltage ($V_F$): 0.5V @ 2.0A
- Package Type: SMA (DO-214AC)
