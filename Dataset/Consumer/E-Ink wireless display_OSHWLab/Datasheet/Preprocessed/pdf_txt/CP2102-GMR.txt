Component ID: CP2102
Component Type: USB-to-UART Bridge Controller (USB Serial Transceiver IC)
Description: The CP2102/9 is a highly-integrated single-chip bridge that converts USB data to asynchronous serial UART data and vice versa. It contains a full-speed USB 2.0 function controller, transceiver, internal oscillator, voltage regulator, and programmable ROM, requiring minimal external components. It enables legacy RS-232 devices and systems to be upgraded to USB by providing a complete UART interface with full modem handshaking signals, appearing as a virtual COM port on the host computer.

Key Parameters:
- USB Specification: USB 2.0 Full-Speed (12 Mbps)
- UART Baud Rate: 300 bps to 1 Mbps
- UART Data Buffer: 576 Byte Receive, 640 Byte Transmit
- Supply Voltage (Self-Powered): 3.0 V to 3.6 V
- Supply Voltage (USB Bus-Powered): 4.0 V to 5.25 V
- Internal Voltage Regulator Output: 3.3 V (CP2102) / 3.45 V (CP2109)
- Internal Programmable ROM: 1024 bytes (EEPROM for CP2102, One-Time Programmable EPROM for CP2109)
- Package: 28-pin QFN (5 mm x 5 mm)
- Operating Temperature Range: –40 °C to +85 °C
