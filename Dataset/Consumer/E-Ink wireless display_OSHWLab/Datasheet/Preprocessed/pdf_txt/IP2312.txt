Component ID: IP2312
Component Type: Single-Cell Li-ion/Li-Polymer Synchronous Buck Charger IC
Description:
The IP2312 is a monolithic synchronous switching buck charger IC designed for single-cell lithium-ion/polymer batteries. It operates from a 5V input, integrates power MOSFETs, and uses a 750kHz switching frequency to achieve high efficiency (up to 94% at 3.7V/2A). It features programmable charging current via an external resistor, supports multiple battery termination voltages (4.2V/4.3V/4.35V/4.4V), and includes comprehensive protection such as input OVP/UVP, NTC temperature monitoring, charge timeout, and IC overtemperature protection. It requires minimal external components and is offered in an ESOP8 package.

Key Parameters:
- Input Voltage Range (Recommended): 4.5V to 5.5V
- Maximum Charging Current: 3 A
- Charging Target Voltage (Typical): 4.2 V (Selectable: 4.2V/4.3V/4.35V/4.4V)
- Switching Frequency (Typical): 750 kHz
- Peak Efficiency: 94% (at VIN=5V, VBAT=3.7V, ICHG=2A)
- Integrated Power MOSFET RDS(ON): PMOS 35 mΩ (typ), NMOS 30 mΩ (typ)
- Trickle Charge Threshold Voltage (Typical): 3.0 V
- Recharge Threshold Voltage (Typical): 4.1 V
- Charge Termination Timeout (Typical): 24 Hours
- Operating Junction Temperature: -40°C to 150°C
