Component ID: KH-TYPE-C-16P
Component Type: USB Type-C Female Connector (Receptacle)
Description: This is a 16-pin, single-row, surface-mount (SMT) USB Type-C receptacle connector with four board-mounting posts. It provides the physical and electrical interface for a USB Type-C plug, supporting power delivery (VBUS), ground (GND), USB 2.0 differential data pairs (D+/D-), configuration channel (CC) pins for cable orientation and power negotiation, and sideband use (SBU) pins for alternate modes like DisplayPort.

Key Parameters:
- Contact Current Rating: 3 A Max
- Rated Voltage: 30 V Max
- Dielectric Withstanding Voltage: AC 100V r.m.s.
- Insulation Resistance: 100 MΩ Minimum
- Contact Resistance: 40 mΩ Maximum
- Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
- Durability Cycles: 10000 Cycles
- Insertion Force: 5 to 20 N
- Withdrawal Force: 6 to 20 N
- Shell Material: Stainless Steel
- Insulator Material: High Temperature Plastic (UL 94 V-0)
- Contact Material: Copper Alloy
