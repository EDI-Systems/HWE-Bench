Component ID: SSB44
Component Type: Surface Mount Schottky Barrier Rectifier Diode
Description: This is a high-current-density, surface-mount Schottky rectifier designed for high-efficiency power conversion. It features a very low forward voltage drop, enabling reduced power losses in applications like DC/DC converters, polarity protection, and freewheeling circuits. It is packaged in an SMB (DO-214AA) case, is RoHS-compliant, and has variants qualified for automotive use (AEC-Q101).

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 30V (SSB43L), 40V (SSB44)
- Maximum Average Forward Rectified Current (IF(AV)): 4.0 A
- Forward Voltage (VF): 0.38V typ., 0.42V max. @ 4.0A, Tj=125°C (SSB44)
- Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 100 A (8.3 ms half-sine wave)
- Maximum Reverse Current (IR): 40 mA max. @ rated VRRM, Tj=125°C (SSB44)
- Operating Junction Temperature (Tj): -65°C to +150°C
- Thermal Resistance, Junction-to-Ambient (RθJA): 70 °C/W (typical)
- Package: SMB (DO-214AA)
