Component ID: TS
Component Type: Tactile Switch (Tact Switch)
Description:
This is a surface-mount, momentary tactile switch designed for low-power signal applications. It functions as a normally-open (NO) push-button switch, closing a circuit between its two terminals when the actuator (keystake) is pressed. The switch features a compact, sealed design with a metal cover and is characterized by its low profile, multiple operating force options, and specified electrical ratings for reliable user interface integration.

Key Parameters:
- Contact Rating (Max Switching): 50 mA, 12 V DC
- Contact Resistance: 100 mΩ max
- Insulation Resistance: 100 MΩ min (at 500 V DC)
- Dielectric Strength: 250 V AC for 1 minute
- Actuator Travel: 0.13 mm
- Operating Force (Options): A: 100 gf, B: 160 gf, C: 260 gf
- Operating Temperature: -30°C to +85°C
- Storage Temperature: -30°C to +80°C
- Package Height (Options): A: 1.8 mm, B: 1.85 mm, C: 1.92 mm, D: 2.0 mm, E: 2.5 mm

Pinout Table:
| Pin Number | Pin Name | Function | Description | Pin Type | Electrical Type |
| ----- | -------- | -------- | ----------- | -------- | --------------- |
| 1 | Terminal 1 | Common / Switch Terminal 1 | One of the two electrical connection points for the switch contact. | SMD Terminal | Signal / Power |
| 2 | Terminal 2 | Common / Switch Terminal 2 | The other electrical connection point for the switch contact. The circuit is closed between Terminal 1 and 2 when the switch is pressed. | SMD Terminal | Signal / Power |
