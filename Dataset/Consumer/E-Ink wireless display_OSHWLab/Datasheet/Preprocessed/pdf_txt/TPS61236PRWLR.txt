Component ID: TPS61236P
Component Type: High-Current Synchronous Boost Converter with Constant Current Output
Description:
The TPS6123x is a family of high-efficiency, synchronous boost converters designed to step up a single-cell Li-Ion/Li-Polymer battery voltage to a higher output. Its core functionality is to provide a regulated, high-current output (up to 3.5A for 3.3V to 5V conversion) with integrated power switches. A key feature is its programmable constant output current capability, which allows it to control power delivery for applications like USB charging without external current-limiting components. It includes comprehensive protection features (soft-start, current limit, OVP, thermal shutdown), offers a low 10µA quiescent current for light-load efficiency, and provides true input-output disconnection during shutdown.

Key Parameters:
- **Topology**: Synchronous Boost Converter
- **Input Voltage Range (VIN)**: 2.3V to 5.5V (from Recommended Operating Conditions context)
- **Output Voltage**: Fixed 5.1V (TPS61235P) or Adjustable 2.9V to 5.5V (TPS61236P)
- **Maximum Output Current**: Up to 3.5A (for 3.3V to 5V conversion)
- **Switch Current Limit (Valley)**: 10A (typ)
- **Internal Switch Resistance (Rds_on)**: 14mΩ (High-side) / 14mΩ (Low-side)
- **Switching Frequency**: 1 MHz (Fixed)
- **Quiescent Current (IQ)**: 10 µA (Light Load)
- **Shutdown Current**: <1 µA
- **Package**: VQFN-9, 2.5mm x 2.5mm
