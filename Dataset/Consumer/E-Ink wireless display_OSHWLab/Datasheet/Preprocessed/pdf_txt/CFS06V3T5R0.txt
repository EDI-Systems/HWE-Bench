Component ID: CFS06
Component Type: Thin Film Chip Fuse (Surface Mount Fuse)
Description: This is a fast-acting, surface-mount thin film fuse designed for overcurrent protection in electronic circuits. It operates by opening the circuit within 5 seconds at 250% of its rated current, thereby protecting downstream components from damage due to overloads or short circuits.

- **Breaking Capacity (Interrupting Rating)**: 35A @ 32VDC or 50A @ 50VDC/35VAC (Specific value depends on part number/series)
- **Maximum Resistance (Rmax)**: 10 mΩ to 350 mΩ (Tolerance ±25%, measured at <10% Irat, specific value depends on part number)
- **Fusing Characteristic (Time-Current)**: Opens within 5 seconds at 250% of rated current.
- **Package Size / Dimensions**: Available in 0402 (CFS04: 1.0x0.52mm), 0603 (CFS06: 1.6x0.8mm), and 1206 (CFS12: 3.1x1.55mm) inch-coded footprints.
