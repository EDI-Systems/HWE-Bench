Component ID: S8050
Component Type: NPN Bipolar Junction Transistor (BJT)
Description: The S 8050S is a general-purpose NPN bipolar junction transistor housed in an SOT-23 surface-mount package. It is designed for amplification and switching applications, capable of handling moderate currents up to 1.5A. Its key functionality is to control a larger collector-emitter current using a smaller base current, with performance characterized by its DC current gain (hFE), saturation voltages, and transition frequency.

Key Parameters:
- Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 25 V (Max)
- Collector Current (Ic): 1.5 A (Max Continuous)
- Collector Power Dissipation (Pc): 300 mW (Max)
- DC Current Gain (hFE1): 120 to 400 (VCE=1V, Ic=100mA)
- Transition Frequency (fT): 100 MHz (Min, VCE=10V, Ic=50MHz)
- Collector-Emitter Saturation Voltage (VCE(sat)): 0.5 V (Max, Ic=800mA, IB=80mA)
- Base-Emitter Saturation Voltage (VBE(sat)): 1.2 V (Max, Ic=800mA, IB=80mA)
- Thermal Resistance (RthJA): 417 °C/W
