# 2.0 mm pitch/Disconnectable Crimp style connectors

![](images/cfd0d1b7379c14557da465b0e780ffeecd2e6bb0e8fc78ae0ec4baa18107baf3.jpg)

This is a thin, low-profile 2.0 mm pitch connector 8.0 mm in height after mounting and 4.5 mm in width. It is designed to meet the demand for high-density connection of internal wires to printed circuit boards. It is compact, highly reliable and low in cost.

● Fully shrouded header
