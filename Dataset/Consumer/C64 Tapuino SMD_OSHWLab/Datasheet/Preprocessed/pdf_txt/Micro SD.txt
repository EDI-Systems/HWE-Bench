Component ID: MICRO
Component Type: SD / Micro SD Card Socket (Connector)
Description: This is a family of surface-mount card sockets designed for secure electrical and mechanical interfacing with SD and Micro SD memory cards. They feature a push-push or push-pull card ejection mechanism for easy insertion and removal, are shielded for EMI/ESD protection, and are compatible with high-speed UHS-I and UHS-II interfaces. Designed for space-constrained portable and automotive applications, they support a second reflow process for double-sided PCB assembly.

Key Parameters:
- Card Type Compatibility: SD, Micro SD (specific to part number)
- Interface Standard: UHS-I, UHS-II
- Number of Positions (Pins): 8 (Micro SD), 9 (SD)
- Current Rating: 0.5A
- Voltage Rating: 5V AC
- Contact Resistance: 100mΩ max.
- Insertion/Extraction Force: 14N max.
- Mechanical Durability: 10,000 cycles
- Operating Temperature Range: -55°C to +85°C (based on environmental test)
- Package/Reel: Tape and Reel
