Component ID: RCLAMP0524P
Component Type: TVS Diode Array (ESD Protection Device)
Description:
The RCLAMP0524P is an ultra-low capacitance, 5V-rated TVS diode array designed for high-speed data line protection. It provides robust, multi-channel ESD, EFT, and surge protection for sensitive interfaces by clamping transient overvololtages to a safe level. Its primary function is to shunt harmful electrical transients from IEC standards to ground, thereby protecting downstream integrated circuits in applications like HDMI, DVI, PCIe, and SATA.

Key Parameters:
- Reverse Working Voltage (VRWM): 5 V (Max)
- Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 6.8 to 9.5 V (IT = 1mA)
- Clamping Voltage (VC): 17 V (Max, IPP = 4.5A, 8/20µs)
- Peak Pulse Current (Ipp): 4.5 A (8/20µs)
- ESD Protection Level (IEC 61000-4-2): ±25 kV (Air), ±20 kV (Contact)
- Junction Capacitance (CJ): 0.35 pF (Typ, I/O to I/O), 0.6 to 0.8 pF (I/O to GND)
- Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 0.08 µA (Max, VRWM = 5V)
- Operating Temperature Range (TJ): -55 to +125 °C
- Package: DFN2510-10 (2.5mm × 1.0mm × 0.5mm)
