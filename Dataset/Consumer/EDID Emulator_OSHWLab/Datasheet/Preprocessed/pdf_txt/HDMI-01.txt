Component ID: HDMI-01
Component Type: HDMI Type-A Female Connector
Description: The HDMI-01 is a standard High-Definition Multimedia Interface (HDMI) Type-A receptacle. It is designed to transmit high-speed digital video and multi-channel audio data using Transition-Minimized Differential Signaling (TMDS). The connector includes three differential data pairs, one differential clock pair, and auxiliary signals for display identification (DDC), device control (CEC), and connection detection (Hot Plug Detect).

Key Parameters:
- Contact Resistance: 30 mΩ Max.
- Mating Force: 44.1 N Max.
- Unmating Force: 9.8 N Min.
- Operating Life: 10000 Cycles Min.
- Storage Temperature Range: -40°C ~ +85°C
- Operating Temperature Range: -25°C ~ +70°C
- Housing Material: LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer), Gray, High-Temperature Plastic
- Contact Material: Brass (H65), Thickness 0.20mm
- Shell Material: Cold Rolled Iron (SPCC), Thickness 0.50mm, Nickel Plated
- Terminal Plating: Contact Area - Gold Plated (2.0μ" min), Solder Area - Tin Plated
