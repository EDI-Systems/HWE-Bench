Component ID: HT42B534-2
Component Type: USB to UART Bridge Controller
Description: The HT42B534-2 is a high-performance USB to UART bridge controller designed by Holtek. It provides a cost-effective and simple solution for bridging USB to asynchronous serial data (UART) interfaces. It integrates a USB 2.0 Full Speed transceiver, an internal oscillator, and a 3.3V LDO regulator, which eliminates the need for an external crystal and reduces the overall bill of materials. It is widely used in USB-to-serial modules, device debugging interfaces, and various industrial control systems.


Key Parameters:
- USB Specification: USB 2.0 Full Speed (12 Mbps) compliant
- UART Baud Rate: Supports standard rates from 300 bps to 3 Mbps
- Operating Voltage: 4.0V to 5.5V (at VDD pin)
- Integrated LDO: 3.3V output for I/O power and external components
