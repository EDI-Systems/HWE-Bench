Component ID: BAT54
Component Type: Schottky Barrier Diode
Description: The BAT54 series are surface-mount Schottky barrier diodes in an SOT23 package, offering a low forward voltage drop and fast switching characteristics. They are designed with a PN junction guard ring for enhanced transient and ESD protection, are fully RoHS compliant, and are qualified for automotive applications (AEC-Q101). The series includes common cathode (BAT54S), series (BAT54), common anode (BAT54A), and series-connected (BAT54C) configurations.

Key Parameters:
- Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 30 V
- Average Rectified Forward Current (Io): 200 mA
- Maximum Forward Voltage (VFmax): 0.8 V @ Io=200mA
- Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IRmax): 2 µA @ VRRM=30V
- Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 300 mA
- Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 600 mA (t < 1.0s)
- Package: SOT23
- Operating Temperature (TA): +25°C (specified for key parameters)
- Qualification: AEC-Q101
