# 3-TERMINAL 0.1A POSITIVE VOLTAGE REGULATORS

# DESCRIPTION

The 78LXX series of fixed voltage monolithic integrated circuit voltage regulators are suitable for applications that required supply up to 100mA.

# FEATURE

\*Maximum output current of 100mA   
\*Output voltage of 5V,6V,8V,9V,10V,12V,15V and 24V   
\*Thermal overload protection   
\*Short circuit current limiting
