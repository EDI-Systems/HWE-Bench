Component ID: INA219
Component Type: High-Side Current and Power Monitor with I2C Interface
Description: The INA219 is a digital current and power monitor with an I2C- or SMBus-compatible interface. The device monitors both shunt voltage drop and bus supply voltage, with programmable conversion times and filtering. A combined internal multiplier, along with an external shunt resistor, enables direct readouts of current in amperes and power in watts.

Key Parameters:
- Bus Voltage Range: 0V to 26V
- Operating Voltage: 3.0V to 5.5V
- Shunt Voltage Range: ±40mV to ±320mV
- Interface: I2C (Supports up to 16 addresses)
