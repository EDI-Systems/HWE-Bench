Component ID: 78L05
Component Type: Positive Linear Voltage Regulator (Low-Dropout, LDO)
Description:
The 78LXX series are monolithic integrated circuit 3-terminal positive voltage regulators designed to provide a fixed, stable output voltage from a higher, unregulated input voltage. They are suitable for low-power applications requiring up to 100mA of supply current and include built-in protection features such as thermal overload shutdown and short-circuit current limiting.

Key Parameters:
- **Maximum Output Current (Io max)**: 100 mA
- **Fixed Output Voltages (Vo)**: 5V, 6V, 8V, 9V, 10V, 12V, 15V, 24V (specific to variant, e.g., 78L05 = 5V)
- **Maximum Input Voltage (Vi max)**: 30V (for Vo=5V, 8V variants); 35V (for Vo=12V, 15V, 24V variants)
- **Dropout Voltage (Vd)**: Typical 1.7V (for 78L05 at Tj=25°C)
- **Line Regulation (ΔVo)**: Typical 8 mV (7V ≤ Vi ≤ 20V for 78L05)
- **Load Regulation (ΔVo)**: Typical 11 mV (Io = 1mA to 100mA for 78L05)
- **Quiescent Current (Iq)**: Typical 2.0 mA, Max 5.5 mA
- **Ripple Rejection (RR)**: Min 40 dB, Typical 49 dB (f=120Hz for 78L05)
- **Operating Junction Temperature (Tj)**: -20°C to +120°C
- **Maximum Power Dissipation (Pd)**: 700 mW
