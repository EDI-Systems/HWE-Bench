Component ID: OLED 0.91
Component Type: 0.91-inch Monochrome OLED Display Module
Description: This is a compact, monochrome graphic OLED (Organic Light-Emitting Diode) display module with a resolution of 128x32 pixels. It is characterized by high contrast and low power consumption since it does not require a backlight. The module is typically powered by the SSD1306 controller IC and communicates with a host microcontroller via a 2-wire I2C serial interface, making it ideal for small wearable devices and status indicators in embedded systems.

Key Parameters:
- Display Resolution: 128 x 32 Pixels
- Interface: I2C (Inter-Integrated Circuit)
- Operating Voltage: 3.3V to 5.0V
- Driver IC: SSD1306
