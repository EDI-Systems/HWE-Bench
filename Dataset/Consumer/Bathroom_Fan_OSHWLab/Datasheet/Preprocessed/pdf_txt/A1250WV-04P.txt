Component ID: A1250
Component Type: Board-to-Wire Connector (4-Pin, A1250WV-04P)
Description: 4-pin connector providing I2C signals (SDA, SCL), ground, and +5 V supply (U5V) for an external module/peripheral.

Pinout Table:

| Pin Number | Pin Name | Function     | Description                                                   | Pin Type     | Electrical Type |
| ---------- | -------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------ | --------------- |
| 1          | SDA      | I2C Data     | I2C serial data line. Requires pull-up to supply on the bus.  | I/O          | Digital         |
| 2          | SCL      | I2C Clock    | I2C serial clock line. Requires pull-up to supply on the bus. | Input        | Digital         |
| 3          | GND      | Ground       | Ground reference. Connect to system ground.                   | Ground       | Power           |
| 4          | U5V      | Power Supply | +5 V supply output to external device.                        | Power Output | Power           |
