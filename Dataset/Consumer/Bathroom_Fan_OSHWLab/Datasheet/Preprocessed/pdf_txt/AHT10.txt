Component ID: AHT10
Component Type: Digital Temperature and Humidity Sensor
Description: The AHT10 is a fully calibrated, digital-output temperature and humidity sensor that communicates via a standard I²C interface. It integrates a MEMS capacitive humidity sensing element and a band-gap temperature sensing element with a custom ASIC for signal processing, providing excellent long-term stability. The device features a low-power sleep mode, rapid response, and strong anti-interference capability, packaged in a 4x5mm SMD footprint suitable for reflow soldering.
