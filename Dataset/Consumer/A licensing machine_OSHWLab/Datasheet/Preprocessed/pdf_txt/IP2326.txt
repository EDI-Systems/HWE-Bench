Component ID: IP2326
Component Type: 2S/3S Li-Ion Battery Boost Charging Management IC
Description: The IP2326 is a highly integrated power management IC designed for charging 2-series or 3-series lithium-ion battery packs from a standard 5V USB input. It features a synchronous boost converter that integrates power MOSFETs to provide high efficiency charging up to 94 percent. The device includes USB Type-C support with CC detection, NTC temperature monitoring, and comprehensive protection features such as overvoltage, undervoltage, and thermal shutdown. It is commonly used in portable devices requiring higher voltage battery packs such as power tools, drones, and high-power portable speakers.

Key Parameters:
- Input Voltage 4.5V to 5.5V
- Battery Configuration 2-series (8.4V) or 3-series (12.6V)
- Maximum Charging Current 1.5A
- Efficiency Up to 94 percent
- Switching Frequency 500 kHz
- Protection OVP, UVLO, OTP, Short Circuit
- Package QFN-24
