Component ID: SS54A
Component Type: Schottky Barrier Rectifier
Description: The SS54A is a surface-mount Schottky barrier rectifier designed for high-efficiency rectification and protection. It features a low forward voltage drop and high switching speed, which helps minimize power loss and improve efficiency in DC-DC converters and power supply circuits. The device is housed in a compact SMA package and includes a guard ring for overvoltage protection and high reliability.

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage 40V
- Maximum Average Forward Current 5.0A
- Peak Forward Surge Current 120A
- Maximum Forward Voltage 0.55V
- Operating Junction Temperature -55 to +150 Celsius
- Package SMA (DO-214AC)
