Component ID: TMC2208
Component Type: Ultra-Silent Stepper Motor Driver
Description The TMC2208 is an ultra-silent motor driver IC for two-phase stepper motors, commonly used in 3D printers and CNC machines. It features StealthChop2 technology for noiseless motor operation and SpreadCycle for high dynamics. It supports a simple Step/Dir interface for motion control and a UART interface for advanced configuration. The module includes an integrated power stage capable of driving up to 1.2A RMS current with microstepping resolution up to 1/256.

Key Parameters:
- Motor Supply Voltage VM: 4.75V to 36V
- Logic Supply Voltage VIO: 3V to 5.25V
- Phase Current: 1.2A RMS
- Peak Current: 2A
- Microstepping Up to 1/256
- Control Interface: Step/Dir or UART
- Thermal Protection Internal thermal shutdown
- Operating Temperature -40 to +125 Celsius
