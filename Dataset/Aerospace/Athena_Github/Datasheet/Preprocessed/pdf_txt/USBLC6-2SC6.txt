Component ID: USBLC6
Component Type: Low Capacitance TVS Diode Array / ESD Protection Device
Description:
This component is a bidirectional, low-capacitance TVS diode array designed to protect two high-speed data lines (e.g., USB D+/D-, Ethernet) from electrostatic discharge (ESD), electrical fast transients (EFT), and surge events. It clamps transient overvoltage to a safe level with minimal impact on signal integrity due to its ultra-low junction capacitance and leakage current, making it suitable for high-speed interfaces operating at 5V.

Key Parameters:
- Reverse Stand-off Voltage (VRWM): 5 V (Max)
- Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 6 V (Min) to 9 V (Max) @ IR=1mA
- Peak Pulse Current (Ipp): 6 A (8/20µs)
- ESD Protection Level (VESD): ±30 kV (Air & Contact, IEC 61000-4-2)
- Clamping Voltage (Vc): 15 V (Max) @ Ipp=6A (8/20µs)
- Junction Capacitance (CJ): 1 pF (Max) @ 0V, 1MHz (I/O to GND)
- Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 0.1 µA (Max) @ VR=5V
- Operating Temperature Range (TJ): -55°C to +125°C
