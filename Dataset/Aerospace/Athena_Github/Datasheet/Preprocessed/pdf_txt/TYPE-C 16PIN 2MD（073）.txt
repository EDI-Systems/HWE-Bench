Component ID: TYPE-C 16PIN 2MD (073)
Component Type: USB Type-C Female Connector (Receptacle)
Description:
This is a 16-pin USB Type-C female connector (receptacle) designed for surface-mount assembly. It functions as a standardized physical and electrical interface for data transfer and power delivery, supporting the reversible plug orientation feature of the USB Type-C standard. The specification details its mechanical durability, electrical performance (including contact resistance and insulation), and environmental reliability to ensure proper mating, signal integrity, and power transmission up to its rated capacity.

Key Parameters:
- Rated Voltage/Current: DC 5V, 5A
- Operating Temperature Range: -25°C to +85°C
- Contact Resistance: ≤ 10 mΩ (measured at ≤100mA)
- Insulation Resistance: ≥ 100 MΩ (at 100V DC)
- Dielectric Strength: Withstands AC 100V (50Hz) for 1 minute
- Insertion Force: 0.5 - 2.0 kgf
- Withdrawal Force: 0.8 - 2.0 kgf
- Mechanical Life (Durability): 5000 mating cycles
- Solder Heat Resistance (Reflow): Peak temperature 260°C ±5°C for ≤20s
- Terminal Strength: Withstands 0.1 N/m (1 kgf/cm) static load for 1 min
