Component ID: BMP388
Component Type: Digital Barometric Pressure Sensor
Description: The BMP388 is a high-performance digital barometric pressure sensor for mobile applications. It offers high accuracy, low power consumption, and low noise, based on Bosch's piezo-resistive pressure sensor technology. It integrates a 24-bit digital output for pressure and temperature via I2C or SPI interfaces. It is ideal for indoor navigation, altitude measurement, and weather forecasting.

Key Parameters:
- Pressure Range 300 to 1250 hPa
- Supply Voltage VDD 1.7V to 3.6V
- I/O Voltage VDDIO 1.2V to 3.6V
- Interface I2C and SPI
- Relative Accuracy +/- 8 Pa
- Temperature Coefficient +/- 0.75 Pa/K
- Operating Temperature -40 to +85 Celsius
- Package 10-pin LGA
