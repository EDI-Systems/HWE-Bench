Component ID: BMI088
Component Type: High Performance Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU)
Description: The BMI088 is a high-performance 6-axis IMU designed for drones, robotics, and high-end consumer applications. It integrates a 16-bit triaxial accelerometer and a 16-bit triaxial gyroscope into a single LGA package. The device offers excellent vibration robustness and high temperature stability. It supports both I2C and SPI digital interfaces and features independent power supplies for the accelerometer and gyroscope parts to optimize power management.

Key Parameters:
- Accelerometer Range +/- 3g, 6g, 12g, 24g
- Gyroscope Range +/- 125, 250, 500, 1000, 2000 deg/s
- Supply Voltage VDD 2.4V to 3.6V
- I/O Voltage VDDIO 1.2V to 3.6V
- Communication I2C (up to 400kHz) and SPI (up to 10MHz)
- Accelerometer Noise 160 ug/sqrt(Hz)
- Gyroscope Noise 0.014 deg/s/sqrt(Hz)
- Operating Temperature -40 to +85 Celsius
- Package LGA-16 (3.0 x 4.5 x 0.95 mm)
