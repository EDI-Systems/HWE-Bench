Component ID: TPS565208
Component Type: Synchronous Buck (Step-Down) Switching Voltage Regulator
Description:
The TPS565208 is a monolithic, 5-A synchronous step-down DC/DC converter in a SOT-23 package. It employs D-CAP2™ mode control for fast transient response without requiring external compensation, supports a 4.5-V to 17-V input range, and is optimized for low external component count and low standby current. It includes integrated power MOSFETs, fixed-frequency operation, and comprehensive protection features.

Key Parameters:
- Input Voltage Range: 4.5 V to 17 V
- Output Voltage Range: 0.76 V to 7 V
- Maximum Continuous Output Current: 5 A
- Switching Frequency: 500 kHz
- Feedback Voltage Accuracy: 1%
- High-Side FET On-Resistance (Rds(on)): 31 mΩ
- Low-Side FET On-Resistance (Rds(on)): 16 mΩ
- Shutdown Current: < 1 µA
- Soft-Start Time: 1.0 ms (Fixed)
- Operating Junction Temperature (Tj): -40°C to 125°C
