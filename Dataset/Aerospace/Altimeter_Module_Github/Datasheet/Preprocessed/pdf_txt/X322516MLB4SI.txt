Component ID: X322516
Component Type: 16MHz SMD Quartz Crystal
Description: The Y1 is a high-precision 16MHz surface-mount quartz crystal resonator housed in a standard 3225 ceramic package. It provides a stable frequency reference for microcontrollers and other digital circuits. The four-pad design includes two terminals for the crystal element and two grounding terminals connected to the metal lid to reduce electromagnetic interference and provide mechanical stability.

Key Parameters:
- Frequency 16.000 MHz
- Package 3225 SMD (3.2mm x 2.5mm)
- Load Capacitance 12pF to 20pF (Typical)
- Frequency Tolerance +/- 20ppm
- Frequency Stability +/- 30ppm
- Equivalent Series Resistance 60 ohm Max
- Operating Temperature -40 to +85 Celsius
