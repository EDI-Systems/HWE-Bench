Component ID: 1N5819
Component Type: Schottky Barrier Diode
Description:
The 1N5819xWS series are Schottky barrier diodes designed for high-efficiency switching applications. They feature a low forward voltage drop, a guard ring construction for transient protection, and exhibit negligible reverse recovery time and low reverse capacitance, making them suitable for fast switching circuits.

Key Parameters:
- Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 20V (CWS), 30V (BWS), 40V (WS)
- Forward Continuous Current (IF(AV)): 350 mA
- Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 1.5 A (t ≤ 1.0s)
- Forward Voltage (VF): 0.37 V (Typ, IF=20mA), 0.60 V (Max, IF=200mA)
- Reverse Current (IR): 5.0 µA (Max, at rated VR)
- Junction Capacitance (CT): 50 pF (Typ, VR=0V, f=1MHz)
- Reverse Recovery Time (trr): 10 ns (Typ)
- Power Dissipation (Pd): 400 mW
- Thermal Resistance (RθJA): 300 °C/W
- Package: SOD-323
