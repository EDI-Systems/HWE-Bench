Component ID: MS561101BA03-50
Component Type: High Resolution Barometric Pressure Sensor
Description: The MS5611 is a high resolution altimeter sensor that includes a high linearity pressure sensor and an ultra low power 24 bit sigma delta ADC with internal factory calibrated coefficients. It provides precise digital 24 bit pressure and temperature values and different operation modes that allow the user to optimize for conversion speed and current consumption. It features both I2C and SPI interfaces for easy communication with microcontrollers and is widely used in flight controllers for drones and handheld altimeters.

Key Parameters:
- Pressure Range 10 to 1200 mbar
- Supply Voltage 1.8V to 3.6V
- Interface I2C and SPI
- ADC Resolution 24 bit
- Altitude Resolution 10 cm
- Accuracy 1.5 mbar at 25 Celsius
- Operating Temperature -40 to +85 Celsius
- Current Consumption 1.4 uA typical
