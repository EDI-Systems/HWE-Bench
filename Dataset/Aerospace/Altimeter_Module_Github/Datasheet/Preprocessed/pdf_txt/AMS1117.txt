Component ID: AMS1117
Component Type: Low Dropout (LDO) Linear Voltage Regulator
Description:
The AMS1117 is a series of three-terminal, positive, low-dropout linear voltage regulators capable of delivering up to 1A of output current. It is available in both adjustable and fixed output voltage versions (1.5V, 1.8V, 2.5V, 2.85V, 3.3V, 5.0V). The device is designed to operate with a very low input-to-output voltage differential, featuring a guaranteed maximum dropout voltage of 1.3V at full load, which decreases at lower currents. It includes on-chip trimming for accurate reference voltage and built-in current limiting for overload protection.

Key Parameters:
- **Output Current (Max)**: 1 A
- **Dropout Voltage (Max)**: 1.3 V (at Iout = 1A, guaranteed)
- **Line Regulation (Max)**: 0.2 %
- **Load Regulation (Max)**: 0.4 %
- **Fixed Output Voltages**: 1.5V, 1.8V, 2.5V, 2.85V, 3.3V, 5.0V
- **Adjustable Reference Voltage**: 1.25 V (Typical)
- **Maximum Input Voltage**: 15 V
- **Operating Junction Temperature**: -40°C to +125°C
- **Available Packages**: SOT-223, TO-252 (DPAK), 8L SOIC
