Component ID: TPM8837C
Component Type: H-Bridge Motor Driver IC
Description:
The TPM8837C is a high-voltage H-bridge driver designed to control inductive loads such as DC motors, solenoids, and relays. It provides bidirectional drive capability with a maximum output current of 1 A from a motor supply voltage (VM) ranging from 2.5 V to 17 V. The device features a PWM control interface (IN1/IN2) compatible with 1.8-V, 3.3-V, and 5-V logic levels and includes integrated protection features like undervoltage lockout (UVLO), over-current protection (OCP), and thermal shutdown (TSD) to enhance system reliability.

Key Parameters:
- Maximum Output Current: 1 A (Continuous)
- Motor Supply Voltage (VM) Range: 2.5 V to 17 V (Operating)
- Logic Supply Voltage (VCC) Compatibility: 1.8 V, 3.3 V, 5 V
- Control Interface: PWM via IN1/IN2
- Protection Features: Undervoltage Lockout (UVLO), Over-Current Protection (OCP), Thermal Shutdown (TSD)
- Package Options: DFN2X2-8L (DFN-8), SOP8
- Operating Junction Temperature Range: -40°C to 150°C
