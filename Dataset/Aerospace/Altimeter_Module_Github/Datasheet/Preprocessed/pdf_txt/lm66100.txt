Component ID: LM66100
Component Type: Integrated Ideal Diode / Power Switch with Reverse Polarity Protection
Description:
The LM66100 is a single-input, single-output (SISO) integrated circuit that functions as an ideal diode using an internal P-channel MOSFET. It provides low-loss power path control with reverse current blocking, reverse polarity input protection, and a chip enable function. It is designed to replace a Schottky diode, offering significantly lower forward voltage drop and power loss, and can be used in ORing configurations for power multiplexing.

Key Parameters:
- Operating Voltage Range (VIN): 1.5 V to 5.5 V
- Maximum Continuous Output Current (IMAX): 1.5 A
- On-Resistance (RON): 79 mΩ (typical at VIN = 5 V)
- Reverse Voltage Standoff on VIN: -6 V (Absolute Maximum)
- Quiescent Current (IQ, VIN): 150 nA (typical at VIN = 3.6 V)
- Shutdown Current (ISD, VIN): 120 nA (typical at VIN = 3.6 V)
- Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to 125°C
- Package: SC-70 (6-pin), Body Size: 2.1 mm x 2.0 mm
