Component ID: G5Q-1A
Component Type: Miniature Power Relay (SPST-NO)
Description: The G5Q-1A is a high-performance, compact PCB-mount power relay manufactured by Omron. It is designed for switching significant loads (up to 10A) in a small footprint, making it ideal for home appliances, HVAC systems, and industrial control modules. This specific "1A" variant features a single-pole, single-throw, normally open contact configuration.

Key Parameters:
- **Contact Form:** SPST-NO (1 Form A)
- **Rated Load (Resistive):** 10A at 125VAC / 5A at 30VDC / 3A at 250VAC
- **Max. Switching Voltage:** 277 VAC / 30 VDC
- **Coil Voltage Options:** 5V, 12V, or 24V DC (model-specific)
