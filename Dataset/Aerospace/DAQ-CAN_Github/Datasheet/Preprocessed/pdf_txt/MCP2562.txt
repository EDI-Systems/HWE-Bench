Component ID: MCP2562
Component Type: High-Speed CAN (Controller Area Network) Transceiver
Description:
The MCP2561/2 is a second-generation, high-speed CAN transceiver that serves as the physical layer interface between a CAN protocol controller and a two-wire differential CAN bus. It supports data rates up to 1 Mb/s, implements ISO-11898-2/5 standards, and is designed for robust automotive applications. Key functionalities include differential transmission and reception, very low standby current, protection against bus faults and transients, and features for system-level management such as a VIO pin for I/O voltage level shifting (MCP2562) or a SPLIT pin for bus common-mode stabilization (MCP2561).

Key Parameters:
- **Data Rate**: Up to 1 Mb/s
- **Supply Voltage (VDD)**: Not explicitly stated in provided text, but implied for 12V/24V systems; typical range for such transceivers is 4.5V to 5.5V.
- **I/O Logic Supply Voltage (VIO)**: 1.8V to 5.5V (MCP2562 feature)
- **Standby Current**: 5 µA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: Extended (E): -40°C to +125°C; High (H): -40°C to +150°C
- **ESD Protection (CANH, CANL)**: ±14 kV (IEC61000-4-2)
- **Fault Protection**: Short-circuit to battery voltage, high-voltage transients, thermal shutdown (~+175°C)
- **Package Options**: PDIP-8, SOIC-8, 3x3 DFN-8
- **Compliance**: ISO-11898-2, ISO-11898-5, Automotive Hardware Requirements
