Component ID: 1N4148WX
Component Type: High-Speed Switching Diode
Description:
The 1N4148WX is a general-purpose, surface-mount silicon switching diode designed for fast switching applications. It features a very low reverse recovery time, making it suitable for high-frequency rectification, signal demodulation, and digital logic circuits. It is housed in a compact SOD-323 package with polarity indicated by a cathode band.

Key Parameters:
- Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRM): 100 V
- Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 150 mA
- Maximum Forward Voltage (VF): 1.0 V @ IF = 50 mA
- Maximum Reverse Recovery Time (trr): 4 ns
- Maximum Reverse Current (IR): 25 nA @ VR=20V, Tj=25°C / 1.0 µA @ VR=75V, Tj=25°C
- Junction Capacitance (Cj): 4 pF (Typical)
- Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 2.0 A (t=1µs)
- Power Dissipation (PD): 200 mW
- Operating Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 to +150 °C
