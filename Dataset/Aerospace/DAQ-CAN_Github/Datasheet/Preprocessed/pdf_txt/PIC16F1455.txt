Component ID: PIC16F1455
Component Type: 8-bit Flash Microcontroller with Full-Speed USB
Description: The PIC16F1455 is a high-performance 8-bit microcontroller from Microchip's Enhanced Mid-range family, featuring eXtreme Low Power (XLP) technology. A standout feature is its "Crystal-less" Full-Speed USB 2.0 interface, which uses an internal oscillator with clock recovery to eliminate the need for an external crystal. It is designed for cost-sensitive and space-constrained applications requiring USB connectivity, such as PC peripherals, medical devices, and industrial loggers.

Key Parameters:
- **Core:** 8-bit PIC (Enhanced Mid-range)
- **Flash Memory:** 14 KB
- **RAM / EEPROM:** 1024 Bytes / (No dedicated EEPROM, uses High-Endurance Flash)
- **Max CPU Speed:** 48 MHz (12 MIPS)
- **USB:** Full-Speed USB 2.0 (12 Mbps) with internal clock recovery
- **Operating Voltage:** 1.8V to 5.5V
- **ADC:** 5-channel, 10-bit resolution
- **Package:** 14-pin PDIP/SOIC/TSSOP (shown in schematic)
