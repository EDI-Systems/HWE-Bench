Component ID: S8050
Component Type: Bipolar Junction Transistor (BJT), NPN
Description: The component is a silicon NPN transistor in a SOT-23 plastic package, designed for power amplifier applications. It functions as a current-controlled switch or amplifier, where a small current at the base terminal controls a larger current flow between the collector and emitter. It features three gain classifications (hFE), with complementary pairing to the S8550M PNP transistor.
