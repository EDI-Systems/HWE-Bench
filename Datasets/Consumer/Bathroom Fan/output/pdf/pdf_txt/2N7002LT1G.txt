Component ID: 2N7002
Component Type: Small Signal N-Channel MOSFET
Description: This is a 60 V, 115 mA N-Channel enhancement-mode MOSFET in a SOT-23 package. It is designed for low-power switching and amplification applications, featuring a typical on-state resistance of 7.5 Ω at 10 V gate drive, a low gate threshold voltage, and qualification for automotive use (AEC-Q101). It is Pb-Free, Halogen Free, and RoHS compliant.
